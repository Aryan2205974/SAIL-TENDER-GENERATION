# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The bids for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, Burgapur, with an Estimated Tender Value of Rs. 29,166,666, shall be opened on the date and time specified by the Tender Committee, in the presence of authorized representatives of the bidders, at the office of the Chief General Manager, Bokaro Steel Plant.
2. The bid opening ceremony shall commence at 10:00 hours, and the bidders or their authorized representatives are required to be present at least 30 minutes prior to the scheduled time to complete the necessary formalities.
3. The Tender Committee, comprising the Chief General Manager, the General Manager (Purchase), and the Deputy General Manager (Materials), shall preside over the bid opening ceremony and ensure that the process is conducted in a fair and transparent manner, with the results being announced immediately after the opening of the bids.

## Bid Submission

1. The bidder shall submit their bid for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, with an Estimated Tender Value of Rs. 29,166,666, in accordance with the terms and conditions specified in the tender document.
2. The bid submission shall be accompanied by an Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000, which shall be submitted either through Payment Gateway or as Bank Guarantee (BG) in the specified format.
3. The bidder shall ensure that their bid is submitted to the Contracts Department, Bokaro Steel Plant, on or before the deadline specified, and the bid shall be considered valid for a period of 120 days from the date of price discovery.
