# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The bids for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, Burgapur, with an Estimated Tender Value of Rs. 29,166,666, shall be opened on the date and time specified by the Tender Committee, in the presence of authorized representatives of the bidders, at the office of the General Manager, Contracts Department, SAIL, Bokaro Steel Plant.
2. The bid opening ceremony shall be conducted by a committee consisting of at least three officers, including the General Manager, Contracts Department, and two other senior officers nominated by the General Manager, to ensure transparency and fairness in the bid opening process.
3. The bids shall be opened in a sequential manner, with the technical bids being opened first, followed by the price bids, and the tenderers or their authorized representatives shall be allowed to witness the bid opening process, with the entire process being video-recorded and retained for a period of at least six months from the date of bid opening.

## Bid Submission

1. The bidder shall submit their bid for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, with an Estimated Tender Value of Rs. 29,166,666, in accordance with the terms and conditions specified in the tender document.
2. The bid submission shall be accompanied by an Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000, which shall be submitted either through Payment Gateway or as Bank Guarantee (BG) in the specified format.
3. The bidder shall ensure that their bid is submitted to the Contracts Department, Bokaro Steel Plant, on or before the deadline specified, and the bid shall be considered valid for a period of 120 days from the date of price discovery.
