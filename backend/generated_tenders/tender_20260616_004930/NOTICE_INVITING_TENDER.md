10. The bid opening shall be conducted at the designated venue as specified in the tender document.
11. Any queries or clarifications regarding the bid opening process should be directed to the Contracts Department.
12. Bidders are advised to ensure that all required documents, including EMD details, are submitted correctly to avoid disqualification during the bid opening process.
