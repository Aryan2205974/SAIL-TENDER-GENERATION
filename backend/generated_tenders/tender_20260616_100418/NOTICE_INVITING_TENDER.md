# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The Techno-Commercial bids shall be opened on 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours by the designated Bid Opening Committee at the Contracts Department, Bokaro Steel Plant. Authorized representatives of the bidders may be present during the opening of the Techno-Commercial bids.

2. The Price Bids of only those bidders who are found techno-commercially qualified shall be opened subsequently. The date and time for the opening of Price Bids shall be intimated separately to the techno-commercially qualified bidders.

3. Bids submitted after the stipulated submission deadline of 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours shall not be accepted and will be returned unopened to the respective bidders.

4. In case of online tenders, the system shall automatically close the bid submission portal at the stipulated deadline. For physical bids, the bids received after the deadline will be returned unopened.

5. The bid opening process for the Techno-Commercial bids will be conducted in the presence of bidders' representatives who choose to attend. Bidders are requested to depute their authorized representatives for witnessing the bid opening.

6. SAIL reserves the right to postpone or reschedule the bid opening date and time for any reason whatsoever, and such revised schedule shall be communicated to the bidders through the SAIL Tender Portal.

7. Any bid not accompanied by the Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000/- or a valid exemption document as per tender conditions shall be summarily rejected. The EMD exemption claims will be verified against the submitted documentation.

8. The Estimated Tender Value for this procurement is Rs. 29,166,666/-. Bidders are advised to submit their offers considering this value and the prevailing market conditions.

## Bid Submission

1.  Bidders shall submit their offers in two parts: Part-I (Techno-Commercial Bid) and Part-II (Price Bid). Both parts must be submitted online through the SAIL Tender Portal (www.sailtenders.co.in) by the stipulated deadline of 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours. Physical submission of bids will not be accepted.
2.  The Techno-Commercial Bid (Part-I) shall contain all technical specifications, commercial terms and conditions, eligibility documents, and any other non-price related information. The Price Bid (Part-II) shall contain only the price schedule in the prescribed format.
3.  Bidders are required to upload scanned copies of all requisite documents in the designated sections of the tender portal. The bid shall be considered incomplete and liable for rejection if any mandatory document or information is found missing.
4.  All bids must be accompanied by an Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs only). The EMD shall be submitted in the form of a Bank Guarantee from a Scheduled Commercial Bank, in the format provided in the tender documents, or through online payment gateway as specified. Bids not accompanied by the EMD or a valid exemption document shall be summarily rejected.
5.  The estimated tender value for this procurement is Rs. 29,166,666/- (Rupees Two Crores Ninety-One Lakhs Sixty-Six Thousand Six Hundred and Sixty-Six only). Bidders are advised to quote their prices considering this value and prevailing market conditions.
6.  Bidders shall ensure that their offers are valid for a minimum period of 120 days from the date of price discovery. Any bid with a shorter validity period shall be liable for rejection.
7.  All bidders must confirm their acceptance of the SAIL S1 Document Version 01, Special Conditions of Contract, Guidelines on Banning of Business Dealings, and Safety Penal Code by uploading a duly signed undertaking. Failure to do so may result in the bid being considered non-responsive.
8.  Bidders are required to submit a duly signed undertaking confirming their adherence to the "Public Procurement (Preference to Make in India)" policy, if applicable, and select the appropriate class of local supplier as per the tender requirements.

## Important Dates

1. The contract for the subject work shall commence on the date of issue of the letter of acceptance, and the contractor shall complete the work within a period of 2 years, considering the estimated tender value of Rs. 29,166,666.
2. Only sealed bids received by 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours shall be considered for evaluation, and late submissions will be returned unopened to the respective bidders.
3. The bid opening committee shall open the techno-commercial bids on 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours, and authorized representatives of the bidders may be present during the opening of the techno-commercial bids.
4. The price bids of only those bidders who are found techno-commercially qualified shall be opened subsequently, and the date and time for the opening of price bids shall be intimated separately to the techno-commercially qualified bidders.
5. The validity period of the tender shall be 120 days from the date of price discovery, and bidders shall ensure that their offers are valid for the entire period.
6. The security deposit for this contract shall be 3% of the order value, excluding taxes and duties, and shall be submitted either through payment gateway or as a bank guarantee.
7. The performance security shall be submitted within 7 days of the issue of the letter of acceptance, and the contractor shall ensure that the security remains valid until the completion of the contract.
8. The completion certificate for the subject work shall be issued within 30 days of the completion of the work, and the contractor shall ensure that all defects are rectified within the defect liability period of 2 years.
