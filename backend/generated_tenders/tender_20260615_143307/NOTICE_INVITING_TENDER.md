# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The Estimated Tender Value (ETV) for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro is Rs. 29,166,666 (approximately Rs. 2.92 Crores).
2. The bids for the above-mentioned procurement shall be opened on the date and time specified in the tender schedule, in the presence of the authorized representatives of the bidders who choose to be present.
3. The bid opening process shall be conducted in a transparent and fair manner, with the bids being opened and read out in the presence of the authorized representatives of the bidders.
4. The bidder or their authorized representative who is present at the bid opening shall be required to sign an attendance sheet to confirm their presence.
5. The bids shall be opened in the order in which they were received, with the earliest received bid being opened first.
6. The name of the bidder, the bid price, and any other relevant details shall be announced and recorded during the bid opening process.
7. In case of any discrepancy or irregularity in the bid, the bidder shall be given an opportunity to clarify or rectify the same, before the bid is considered for further evaluation.
8. The bid opening process shall be videographed and a record of the same shall be maintained by the Purchaser.
9. The Purchaser reserves the right to postpone or cancel the bid opening process, if necessary, and the bidders shall be informed of the same through a notification on the SAIL website.
10. The Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000 shall be returned to the unsuccessful bidders within 30 days of the completion of the bid evaluation process.
11. The successful bidder shall be required to furnish a Performance Security of 10% of the contract value (estimated as per PSU norms) within 7 days of the issue of the Letter of Intent.
12. The contract value shall be based on the L1 price, which shall be determined after the bid evaluation process is completed.
13. The Purchaser shall not be responsible for any costs or expenses incurred by the bidders in connection with the bid opening process.
14. The bid opening process shall be governed by the terms and conditions of the tender document, and the bidders shall be deemed to have accepted the same by participating in the bid opening process.
15. The decision of the Purchaser regarding the bid opening process shall be final and binding on all bidders.

## Bid Submission

1. The Estimated Tender Value (ETV) for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro is Rs. 29,166,666 (approximately Rs. 2.92 Crores), and bidders are required to submit their bids accordingly.
2. Bidders must submit an Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000 along with their bid, which will be returned to the unsuccessful bidders after the award of the contract.
3. The EMD may be submitted in the form of a Bank Guarantee (BG) or through Payment Gateway, and the bidder must ensure that the EMD is valid for a period of at least 120 days from the date of bid submission.
4. Bidders who are exempt from submitting EMD must claim the exemption only if all the three clauses mentioned in the tender are being fulfilled, and Durgapur Steel Plant reserves the right to cancel an offer without EMD if the EMD exemption clause is found not fulfilled.
5. The bid submission deadline is 14:00:00 hours on the deadline date, and bids received after this time will not be considered.
6. Bidders must submit their bids in two parts: Part A (Technical Bid) and Part B (Financial Bid), and both parts must be submitted separately.
7. The technical bid must include all the necessary documents, such as Memorandum of Association, GSTIN, PAN, EPF registration, ESI registration, and Udyam Certificate, as applicable.
8. The financial bid must include the price quote, and the bidder must ensure that the price quote is valid for a period of 120 days from the date of price bid opening.
9. Bidders must also submit a Deviation Statement, if any, along with their bid, and the deviation must be clearly marked and explained.
10. The bidder must ensure that all the documents submitted are complete, correct, and in the prescribed format, and any incomplete or incorrect document may lead to rejection of the bid.
11. The bidder must also submit a Turnover Certificate (without GST) on the Chartered Accountant's letterhead with UDIN enclosed, and the turnover must be at least Rs. 58,333,332 (approximately Rs. 5.83 Crores) (estimated as per PSU norms, considering the ETV and industry standards).
12. The bidder must participate in the tender through an authorized representative, and the representative must provide their name, designation, email, phone number, and PAN number.
13. The bidder must ensure that they have the necessary experience and expertise to execute the contract, and the bidder must have completed at least three similar contracts in the last three years.
14. The bidder must also submit a declaration that they have not been banned from business dealings by any government agency or PSU, and the bidder must comply with all the applicable laws and regulations.
15. The bidder must confirm that they have read, agreed, and accepted all the terms and conditions of the tender, including the Special Conditions of Contract, Safety Penal Provisions, and Guidelines on Banning of Business Dealings, and the bidder must sign and stamp the bid document accordingly.
