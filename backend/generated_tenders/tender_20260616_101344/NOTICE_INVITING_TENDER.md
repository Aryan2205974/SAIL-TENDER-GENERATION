# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The Techno-Commercial bids shall be opened on 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours by the designated Bid Opening Committee in the presence of authorized representatives of the bidders who may choose to attend. The venue for the bid opening shall be the Contracts Department, Durgapur Steel Plant, SAIL.

2. Following the evaluation of Techno-Commercial bids, SAIL shall notify the eligible bidders whose bids have been found technically and commercially acceptable. These qualified bidders will then be invited to participate in the Price Bid opening process, which may involve Reverse Auction (RA) as detailed in the tender document.

3. The Price Bids of only those bidders who are found techno-commercially qualified shall be opened. SAIL reserves the right to discover the L-1 bidder/tenderer amongst the techno-commercially qualified bidders/tenderers by opening the sealed price bids, pre-submitted by such bidders along with the techno-commercial bids on or before the Tender Opening Date (TOD) or by conducting Reverse Auction.

4. Authorized representatives of the bidders attending the Price Bid opening session are required to produce a letter of authorization from their respective organizations, duly signed by a competent authority, clearly indicating their authority to represent the bidder during the bid opening proceedings.

5. Any bid received after the stipulated submission deadline of 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours shall not be considered for evaluation and shall be returned unopened to the bidder, except under circumstances explicitly permitted by SAIL's procurement guidelines.

6. SAIL reserves the right to postpone or reschedule the bid opening date and time for any reason whatsoever. In such an event, all participating bidders shall be duly notified of the revised schedule through official communication channels.

7. The EMD exemption clause, if claimed by a bidder, shall be verified against the three stipulated conditions. Durgapur Steel Plant reserves the exclusive right to cancel any offer without EMD if the EMD exemption clause is found to be not fulfilled by the bidder.

8. Bidders are advised to ensure that all required documents, including the EMD details or proof of exemption, are uploaded or submitted as per the instructions in the tender document prior to the bid submission deadline. Failure to comply may lead to disqualification.

9. The estimated tender value for this procurement is Rs. 29,166,666 (Rupees Two Crores Ninety-One Lakhs Sixty-Six Thousand Six Hundred and Sixty-Six only). This figure serves as an indicative value for the scope of work and associated financial considerations during bid evaluation.

10. The bid opening committee shall meticulously record the details of all bids received, including the bidder's name, EMD amount, and any deviations declared. This record shall form part of the official tender opening minutes.

11. In case of online tenders, the system generated acknowledgement of bid submission shall be considered as proof of submission. For physical bids, a stamped acknowledgement receipt issued by the Contracts Department shall be provided.

12. Bidders are required to submit their offers in two parts: Part-I containing Techno-Commercial bid and Part-II containing Price Bid. Both parts must be submitted by the deadline of 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours.

13. The validity of the submitted bids shall be for a period of 120 days from the date of price discovery, as stipulated in the tender document. Bidders must ensure their price remains valid for this duration.

14. Any queries or clarifications regarding the bid opening process or the tender document should be addressed to the Contracts Department well in advance of the submission deadline.

15. The successful bidder will be required to submit a Security Deposit (SD) of 3% of the Order Value (excluding Taxes and Duties) either through Payment Gateway or as a Bank Guarantee (BG).

## Bid Submission

1.  Bidders shall submit their bids electronically through the SAIL e-procurement portal, www.sailtenders.co.in, in accordance with the two-part bidding system. The Techno-Commercial bid and the Price bid shall be submitted separately and distinctly, ensuring that the Price bid remains encrypted until the designated opening time for qualified bidders. All submitted documents must be digitally signed by the authorized signatory of the bidder.

2.  The Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) for this procurement shall be Rs. 500,000 (Rupees Five Lakhs only). Bidders claiming exemption from EMD submission must satisfy all three stipulated conditions as detailed in the tender document and the retrieved context. Failure to meet any of these conditions will render the EMD exemption invalid, and the bid may be cancelled without further notice.

3.  All bids must be submitted by the stipulated deadline of 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours. Any bid received after this time, irrespective of the reason, shall be treated as a late submission and will be returned unopened to the bidder. This strict adherence to the submission deadline is mandatory for all participants.

4.  The total estimated value of this procurement is Rs. 29,166,666 (Rupees Two Crores Ninety-One Lakhs Sixty-Six Thousand Six Hundred and Sixty-Six only). Bidders are advised to ensure their pricing is competitive and reflects the scope of work for the Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application.

5.  Bidders are required to submit a comprehensive set of supporting documents along with their Techno-Commercial bid. This includes, but is not limited to, GSTIN, PAN, EPF registration, ESI registration, and a UDIN-certified Turnover Certificate (without GST) on CA Letterhead for the last three financial years ending 31st March 2025.

6.  The validity of the submitted bids shall be for a period of 120 days from the date of price discovery. Bidders must ensure that their offered prices remain firm and valid for this entire duration, and any bid with a shorter validity period may be considered non-responsive.

7.  Bidders must provide documentary evidence of successful completion of similar works. Specifically, they must have completed either three similar works each costing not less than Rs. 48,58,876/- or two similar works each costing not less than Rs. 60,73,595/- or one similar work costing not less than Rs. 97,17,751/-, all exclusive of GST, within the last seven years ending the last day of the month preceding the NIT invitation.

8.  The average annual financial turnover (exclusive of GST) of the bidder during the last three financial years ending 31st March 2025, should be at least Rs 36,44,157/-. Proof of this turnover must be submitted in the form of a CA certificate with UDIN.

9.  Bidders must confirm their acceptance of the "Guidelines on Banning of Business Dealings" (Revision 2, effective from 04.05.2024), which are available on the SAIL Tender website. Any adverse report against a bidder concerning capabilities or performance within the last three years preceding the NIT date may lead to bid rejection as per Clause 6.11 of SAIL S1 Document Version 01.

10. All submitted documents, including the bid itself, must be in English. If any supporting document is in a language other than English, it must be accompanied by a certified English translation. The bidder shall be solely responsible for the accuracy and authenticity of the translated documents.

11. Bidders are required to upload a duly filled and signed Questionnaire along with all necessary attachments as specified in the tender document. This includes details regarding MSE status, UDYAM registration, and confirmation of acceptance of various SAIL policies and conditions.

12. The bidder must provide the contact details of the person authorized to participate in the Reverse Auction (RA) process, including their Name, Designation, Email, Phone Number, PAN Number, and Address. This information is crucial for communication and participation in the price discovery mechanism.
