# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The bids for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro, with an Estimated Tender Value of Rs. 29,166,666, shall be opened on the date and time specified by the Tender Opening Committee, in the presence of authorized representatives of the bidders, at the office of the General Manager (Purchase), Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro.
2. The Tender Opening Committee shall verify the completeness and validity of the bids, including the Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000, and ensure that all bids are properly sealed and labeled before proceeding with the bid opening.
3. The bid opening process shall be conducted in a transparent and fair manner, with the names of the bidders, bid prices, and other relevant details being announced and recorded, and the proceedings being witnessed by the authorized representatives of the bidders, to ensure the integrity of the procurement process.
