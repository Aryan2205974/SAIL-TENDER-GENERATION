# ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

## Disqualification Criteria

1. The bidder shall be disqualified if they fail to meet the minimum work experience requirement of 2 years in the supply of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application.
2. Any bidder who has been blacklisted or debarred by SAIL or any other government organization shall be disqualified from participating in this tender for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks with an Estimated Tender Value of Rs. 29,166,666.
3. Bidders who have pending litigations or court cases with SAIL shall be disqualified, unless they provide a valid court order or settlement agreement to the satisfaction of SAIL.
4. The bidder shall be disqualified if they fail to submit the required documents, including proof of experience, financial statements, and technical specifications, within the stipulated timeframe.
5. Any bidder who is found to have provided false or misleading information in their bid shall be disqualified and may face further action as per the provisions of the Indian Contract Act.
6. Bidders who have been convicted of any offense related to business practices, such as fraud, corruption, or bribery, shall be disqualified from participating in this tender.
7. The bidder shall be disqualified if they are found to be in breach of any contractual obligations with SAIL or any other government organization, including non-payment of dues or non-compliance with contractual terms.
8. Any bidder who attempts to influence the tendering process through unfair means, such as bribery, coercion, or collusion, shall be disqualified and may face further action as per the provisions of the Indian Penal Code.
9. The bidder shall be disqualified if they fail to comply with the requirements of the GeM portal or the SAIL tendering process, including non-submission of online bids or non-payment of the requisite fees.
10. SAIL reserves the right to disqualify any bidder at any stage of the tendering process if it is found that the bidder does not meet the eligibility criteria or has failed to comply with the terms and conditions of the tender.
