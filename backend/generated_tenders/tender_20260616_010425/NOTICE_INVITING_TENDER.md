# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The Techno-Commercial bids shall be opened on 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours by the designated Bid Opening Committee at the Contracts Department, Bokaro Steel Plant. Authorized representatives of the bidders, holding valid authorization letters, may be present during the opening of the Techno-Commercial bids.

2. Price bids of only those bidders who are found techno-commercially qualified shall be opened on a subsequent date, which will be intimated to the qualified bidders separately. SAIL reserves the right to conduct Reverse Auction (RA) for price discovery amongst the techno-commercially qualified bidders.

3. Any bid received after the submission deadline of 14.03.2026 at 14:00 hours shall be considered late and will not be accepted for evaluation. This includes bids submitted physically or electronically.

4. Bidders are required to submit the Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) amounting to Rs. 500,000/- (Rupees Five Lakhs only) in the acceptable modes as specified in the tender document. Exemption from EMD submission shall be granted only if all three stipulated clauses for exemption are fulfilled, and documentary evidence must be uploaded accordingly. Durgapur Steel Plant reserves the right to cancel an offer without EMD if the exemption clause is found not fulfilled.

5. The bid opening shall be conducted in accordance with the provisions of SAIL's Standard Documents and relevant GFR guidelines. Any adverse report received against a bidder concerning capabilities and/or performance during the last three years preceding the last day of the month in which the NIT is invited may lead to rejection of the bid.

6. Bidders are required to upload all necessary documents, including GSTIN, PAN, EPF registration, ESI registration, and Udyam Certificate, as proof of eligibility and for any claimed exemptions. The Udyam Registration will be verified online from the Udyam Registration Portal.

7. The validity of the bids shall be for a period of 120 days from the date of price discovery. Bidders are requested to ensure their offer remains valid for this period.
