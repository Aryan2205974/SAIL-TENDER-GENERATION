# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The bids for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro, with an Estimated Tender Value of Rs. 29,166,666, shall be opened on the date and time specified in the tender schedule.
2. The bid opening process shall be conducted in the presence of the authorized representatives of SAIL, and the bidders or their authorized representatives may also attend the bid opening.
3. The bids shall be opened in two parts: the technical bid and the price bid, and the technical bids shall be opened first.
4. The technical bids shall be evaluated to determine the eligibility and technical suitability of the bidders, and only the technically qualified bidders shall be allowed to participate in the price bid opening.
5. The price bids of the technically qualified bidders shall be opened, and the bids shall be compared to determine the lowest bidder.
6. In case of a tie, the bidder who has quoted the lowest price shall be considered the lowest bidder, and in case of a further tie, the bidder who has submitted their bid earliest shall be considered the lowest bidder.
7. The bidder shall submit an Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000, which shall be returned to the unsuccessful bidders after the award of the contract.
8. The EMD shall be submitted through a payment gateway or as a Bank Guarantee (BG), and the bidder shall upload the online acknowledgement generated from the payment gateway or a copy of the BG along with their bid.
9. The bidder shall ensure that their bid is valid for a period of 120 days from the date of bid opening, and the bidder shall extend the validity of their bid if required by SAIL.
10. The bidder shall not be allowed to withdraw their bid after the bid opening, and any attempt to do so shall render the bid ineligible for consideration.
11. SAIL reserves the right to reject any or all bids without assigning any reason, and the decision of SAIL shall be final and binding on all bidders.
12. The bid opening process shall be conducted in a fair and transparent manner, and SAIL shall ensure that all bidders are treated equally and without any bias or prejudice.
13. The bidder shall comply with all applicable laws, rules, and regulations, including those related to anti-bribery and corruption, and shall submit an anti-bribery undertaking along with their bid.
14. The bidder shall provide all necessary documentation and information as required by SAIL, and shall cooperate fully with SAIL during the bid evaluation and contract award process.
15. The contract shall be awarded to the lowest bidder who has fulfilled all the eligibility and technical requirements, and who has submitted a compliant bid, and the contract shall be signed within 30 days of the award of the contract.

## Bid Submission

1. The bidder shall submit their bid for the procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application of Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro, with an Estimated Tender Value of Rs. 29,166,666, in accordance with the terms and conditions specified in this Notice Inviting Tender.
2. The bid submission shall be made online through the designated e-procurement portal, and the bidder shall ensure that their bid is submitted before the deadline specified in the tender schedule.
3. The Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) for this tender is Rs. 500,000, which shall be submitted by the bidder along with their bid, except in cases where exemption from EMD submission is claimed and fulfilled as per the tender conditions.
4. The bidder shall upload all the required documents, including the technical bid, price bid, and other supporting documents, as specified in the tender schedule, to the e-procurement portal.
5. The bidder shall ensure that their bid is complete in all respects, including all the required documents and information, and that it is submitted in the prescribed format.
6. The bidder shall not submit more than one bid for this tender, and any attempt to submit multiple bids shall render all bids submitted by the bidder liable for rejection.
7. The bidder shall ensure that their bid is valid for a period of 120 days from the date of price discovery, as specified in the tender schedule.
8. The bidder shall submit their price bid in Indian Rupees (INR), and the price quoted shall be inclusive of all taxes, duties, and other levies.
9. The bidder shall not quote any conditional or differential prices, and the price quoted shall be firm and fixed for the entire duration of the contract.
10. The bidder shall ensure that their bid is signed by an authorized representative of the bidder, and that the signature is duly authenticated.
11. The bidder shall submit a scanned copy of the signed bid document, along with all the required documents, to the e-procurement portal.
12. The bidder shall ensure that their bid is submitted from a valid and authorized location, and that the bid is not submitted from a location that is prohibited or restricted by law.
13. The bidder shall be responsible for ensuring the confidentiality and security of their bid, and shall not share or disclose any information related to their bid with any third party.
14. The bidder shall be liable for any errors or omissions in their bid, and shall be responsible for correcting any such errors or omissions at their own cost.
15. The bidder shall comply with all the terms and conditions specified in this Notice Inviting Tender, and shall be bound by the decisions of the tendering authority in all matters related to the tender.
