# INSTRUCTIONS TO BIDDERS

## Amendments

1. Amendments to the Tender Terms and Conditions:

In accordance with the provisions of the Government of India's General Financial Rules (GFR) 2017, the following amendments are made to the tender terms and conditions as per the requirements of SAIL.

2. Changes in Estimated Tender Value:

The estimated tender value for this procurement has been revised from Rs. 29,166,666 to Rs. 375,000,000, as per the updated Company Statistics block above.

3. Update on EMD Waiver:

The Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) waiver condition is hereby amended to require a scanned copy of the supporting document for EMD waiver, in addition to the mandatory submission of Annexure-S and Annexure BDFV01.

4. Amendment to Completion Period:

The completion period for this project has been revised from 2 years to 3 years, as per the updated Company Statistics block above.

5. Update on Performance Security:

The performance security amount has been increased from Rs. 500,000 to Rs. 1,000,000, as per the updated RETRIEVED TENDER CONTEXT block above.

6. Amendment to Bid Submission Requirements:

Tenders must be submitted electronically through the DSP SRM website (https://srm.saildsp.co.in) and must include all required documents in the Technical Rfx Response folder only.

## Award of Contract

1. The Contract shall be awarded to the successful bidder in accordance with the provisions of the Government of India's General Financial Rules (GFR) 2017, as amended from time to time, and in compliance with the terms and conditions specified in this Tender Document.

2. The Contract shall be executed for a period of 2 years from the date of award, subject to satisfactory completion of the work by the successful bidder.

3. The successful bidder shall pay an Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000 (Five Hundred Thousand Rupees) within timeframe, e.g., "three days" of receipt of this Tender Document.

4. The Contract value is estimated to be Rs. 29,166,666 (Twenty-Nine Crores One Hundred Sixty-Six Lacs Sixty-Six Thousand Rupees), and the successful bidder shall ensure that all payments are made in accordance with the payment terms specified in this Tender Document.

5. The successful bidder shall provide a Performance Security of amount (Rupees) to be deposited within timeframe, e.g., "three days" of receipt of this Tender Document.

6. The Contract shall be subject to satisfactory completion of all technical and financial requirements as specified in this Tender Document, and the successful bidder shall ensure that all work is carried out in accordance with the approved Technical Specifications for Refractory Materials.
