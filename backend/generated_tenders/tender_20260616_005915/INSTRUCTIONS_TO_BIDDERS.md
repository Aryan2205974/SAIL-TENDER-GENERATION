# INSTRUCTIONS TO BIDDERS

## Amendments

1.  Any addendum, corrigendum, or extension of the Bid Submission Deadline or Bid Opening Date pertaining to this procurement shall be published exclusively on the official SAIL e-procurement portals, namely https://srm.saildsp.co.in and https://sailtenders.co.in. Bidders are solely responsible for regularly monitoring these websites for any such updates.

2.  All amendments, if any, shall be considered an integral part of the tender documents. Bidders shall be required to acknowledge receipt and acceptance of all issued amendments prior to or at the time of submitting their bids. Failure to do so may render the bid liable for rejection.

3.  The Bid Opening Committee reserves the right to amend the tender documents at any stage prior to the Bid Submission Deadline. Such amendments will be communicated to all prospective bidders through the aforementioned e-procurement portals.

4.  Bidders shall ensure that their uploaded bid documents reflect all amendments issued up to the Bid Submission Deadline. Any discrepancy between the bid submitted and the latest amendments may lead to disqualification.

5.  In the event of any amendment affecting the scope of work, technical specifications, or commercial terms, the Bid Submission Deadline may be extended at the discretion of SAIL. Any such extension will be duly notified on the SAIL e-procurement portals.

6.  For vendors registered on the DSP SRM portal, it is imperative to utilize their existing User ID and Password for accessing and responding to any amendments. The process for generating new credentials, if required, is detailed on the portal.

7.  Amendments may include, but are not limited to, clarifications on tender clauses, modifications to eligibility criteria, changes in quantities, or revisions to the estimated tender value, which is currently estimated at Rs. 29,166,666.

## Award of Contract

1.  The successful bidder shall be issued a Purchase Order (PO) or a Contract, as deemed appropriate by SAIL, for the entire quantity of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS-II Ladle application. The estimated tender value for this procurement is Rs. 29,166,666.
2.  SAIL reserves the right to award the contract to a single bidder or split the award among multiple bidders, based on the evaluation of bids and the best interest of the company.
3.  The Purchase Order/Contract shall be issued to the bidder whose bid is found to be technically acceptable and commercially lowest, subject to fulfillment of all tender conditions and eligibility criteria.
4.  The successful bidder shall be required to submit a Performance Security within fifteen (15) days of the receipt of the Purchase Order/Letter of Acceptance. The Performance Security shall be equivalent to ten percent (10%) of the total contract value, to be submitted in the form of a Bank Guarantee from a Scheduled Bank, valid for the entire contract period plus sixty (60) days beyond the warranty period.
5.  SAIL reserves the right to accept or reject any bid, or to annul the bidding process and reject all bids, at any time prior to the award of contract, without thereby incurring any liability to the affected bidder or bidders, or any obligation to inform the affected bidder or bidders of the grounds for SAIL's action.
6.  The successful bidder shall be required to enter into a formal contract with SAIL, incorporating all terms and conditions stipulated in the tender documents, the bid submitted by the bidder, and any subsequent clarifications or amendments agreed upon.
7.  Any deviation from the terms and conditions of the awarded contract, including but not limited to delivery schedules, quality specifications, or payment terms, shall render the contract liable for termination and forfeiture of the Performance Security.
8.  The contract shall be awarded for a completion period of 2 months from the date of issue of the Purchase Order/Letter of Acceptance.

## Bid Evaluation

1.  The bids shall be evaluated by a duly constituted Bid Opening Committee and/or Bid Evaluation Committee appointed by SAIL. The evaluation process shall strictly adhere to the terms and conditions stipulated in the tender documents, including the eligibility criteria, technical specifications, and commercial terms.

2.  The evaluation shall be conducted in two stages: first, the technical bids shall be opened and evaluated to ascertain their conformity with the specified technical requirements and eligibility criteria. Only those bids found technically acceptable shall be considered for the second stage of evaluation.

3.  Subsequently, the price bids of only those bidders whose technical bids have been found acceptable shall be opened and evaluated. The commercially lowest responsive bid, determined after considering all applicable taxes, duties, and other charges, shall be identified.

4.  SAIL reserves the right to seek clarifications from bidders on any aspect of their bid during the evaluation process. Such clarifications, if sought, shall be provided in writing and shall not involve any change in the substance of the bid or the price quoted. Failure to provide satisfactory clarifications within the stipulated timeframe may lead to the bid being considered non-responsive.

5.  The evaluation of bids shall be based on the total landed cost to Bokaro Steel Plant, inclusive of all taxes, duties, freight, insurance, and any other charges. The estimated tender value for this procurement is Rs. 29,166,666.

6.  SAIL shall have the right to conduct pre-award negotiations with the lowest responsive bidder(s) to seek further reductions in prices or improvements in terms and conditions, without altering the scope of the work or the fundamental aspects of the bid.

7.  The Bid Evaluation Committee shall prepare a comparative statement of technically acceptable and commercially viable bids. The final recommendation for award of contract shall be based on this evaluation, subject to the approval of the competent authority within SAIL.

8.  SAIL reserves the right to reject any or all bids, or to annul the bidding process at any stage without assigning any reason whatsoever, and without thereby incurring any liability to the affected bidder(s).

## Bid Security

1.  All bidders shall furnish an Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 500,000 (Rupees Five Lakhs only) along with their bid. This EMD is a mandatory requirement for bid validity and shall be submitted in the form of a Demand Draft/Banker's Cheque/Bank Guarantee from a Scheduled Bank, drawn in favour of "SAIL, Bokaro Steel Plant", payable at Bokaro Steel City. The EMD shall remain valid for a period of forty-five (45) days beyond the bid validity period.

2.  The Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) shall be submitted in the prescribed format and uploaded in the designated Technical RFx Response folder within the SRM portal. Failure to submit the EMD in the acceptable form and within the stipulated timeframe shall render the bid liable for rejection without any further reference to the bidder.

3.  The EMD of unsuccessful bidders shall be returned promptly after the finalization of the award of contract, without any interest. For the successful bidder, the EMD shall be adjusted towards the Performance Security deposit, or refunded after the successful submission of the Performance Security, as per the terms and conditions of the contract.

4.  No interest shall be paid on the Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) furnished by the bidders.

5.  In case of withdrawal of bid or modification of bid by any bidder after the bid submission deadline but before the expiry of the bid validity period, the Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) furnished by such bidder shall be forfeited.

6.  The Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) shall also be forfeited if the successful bidder fails to commence the supply of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application or fails to furnish the Performance Security within the stipulated period as per the terms of the contract.

7.  Bidders seeking exemption from payment of EMD must submit valid documentary proof of their registration as a Micro and Small Enterprise (MSE) or any other eligible category as per Government of India guidelines, along with their bid. Such exemption shall be considered strictly in accordance with prevailing Government of India policies and SAIL's internal guidelines.
