# NOTICE INVITING TENDER

## Bid Opening

1. The Bid Opening will commence on [14.03.2026 14:00:00] at the Steel Authority of India Limited, Durgapur Steel Plant, and all interested bidders are required to be present in person.

2. All bidders must submit their bids in the prescribed format, along with the techno-commercial bids, by [14.03.2026 14:00:00].

3. The sealed price bids, pre-submitted by such bidders along with the techno-commercial bids, will be opened by SAIL on [14.03.2026 14:00:00] or as per the discovery of price strategy intimated to techno-commercially acceptable bidders.

11. The EMD amount of Rs. 500,000 will be refunded to the winning bidder upon completion of the work within the stipulated timeframe.

12. The warranty period for the work will be as per the bidder's best offer submitted during the tender process.

13. The bidder who wins the contract is required to provide a completion certificate within [30 days] from the date of completion of the work.

14. Any deviation from the agreed-upon terms and conditions will result in penalty rates as per Clause 6.11 of SAIL S1 Document Version 01.

15. The winning bidder must ensure that all materials used for the work comply with the specifications mentioned in the tender document.
