**Testing Methodology**  

1. The supplier shall conduct chemical analysis of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks as per IS 1528 (Part 12) or equivalent international standards.  

2. Physical properties, including bulk density, apparent porosity, and cold crushing strength, shall be tested in accordance with IS 1528 (Part 3) or ASTM C133.  

3. Refractoriness under load (RUL) testing shall be performed as per IS 1528 (Part 8) or ASTM C16.  

4. Thermal shock resistance shall be evaluated through cyclic heating and quenching tests following IS 1528 (Part 13) or ASTM C117.  

5. Petrographic analysis shall be conducted to determine microstructure and phase composition in accordance with ASTM C856.  

6. Dimensional accuracy checks shall be carried out using calibrated measuring instruments as per IS 1528 (Part 2).  

7. The supplier shall provide certified test reports from NABL-accredited laboratories for all specified tests.  

8. Random sampling for quality verification shall be conducted as per AQL 2.5, with samples drawn from each production batch.  

9. Rejection criteria shall be based on non-conformance with specified technical parameters in the tender.  

10. Retesting of disputed samples shall be performed at an independent laboratory mutually agreed upon by both parties.
