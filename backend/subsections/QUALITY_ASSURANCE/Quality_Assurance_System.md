**QUALITY ASSURANCE SYSTEM**  

1. The bidder shall maintain a documented Quality Assurance (QA) system compliant with ISO 9001 or equivalent standards.  

2. The QA system shall cover raw material procurement, production, testing, and dispatch processes for Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks.  

3. The bidder shall provide a valid certificate of accreditation from a recognized national or international body for their QA system.  

4. The bidder shall submit a detailed QA plan outlining control measures for chemical composition, physical properties, and dimensional accuracy.  

5. The QA plan shall include process flowcharts indicating inspection and testing stages.  

6. The bidder shall ensure traceability of materials through batch/lot identification from raw material to finished product.  

7. Non-conforming products shall be segregated and handled as per documented corrective action procedures.  

8. The bidder shall furnish records of internal audits and management reviews conducted within the last 12 months.  

9. The bidder shall provide training records of personnel involved in QA activities.  

10. Any deviations from specified QA requirements shall be reported with justification and mitigation measures.
