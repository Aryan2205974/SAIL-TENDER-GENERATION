**3. Quality Documentation**  

3.1 The bidder shall submit a detailed quality assurance plan (QAP) outlining the methodology for ensuring compliance with specified technical parameters.  

3.2 Manufacturer’s test certificates for chemical composition, physical properties, and refractory characteristics shall be furnished for each consignment.  

3.3 The bidder shall provide certified mill test reports (MTRs) from an accredited laboratory for raw materials used in production.  

3.4 Batch-wise inspection reports, including dimensional checks and visual examination records, shall be submitted prior to dispatch.  

3.5 Documentation for traceability, including heat numbers, manufacturing dates, and packing details, shall be maintained and shared upon request.  

3.6 Third-party inspection certificates, if applicable, shall be provided as per the purchaser’s requirement.  

3.7 The bidder shall ensure all documents are legible, stamped, and signed by authorized personnel.  

3.8 Non-conformance reports (NCRs) and corrective action records, if any, shall be disclosed.  

3.9 The bidder shall confirm adherence to relevant ISO or equivalent quality management system standards.  

3.10 Any additional quality documentation mandated by the purchaser shall be submitted within the stipulated timeframe.
