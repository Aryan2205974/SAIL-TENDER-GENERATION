**Inspection And Verification**  

1. The Supplier shall permit the Purchaser or its authorized representative to inspect and verify the manufacturing process, quality control measures, and testing facilities at the Supplier’s works or sub-supplier’s premises.  

2. All inspections and verifications shall be conducted as per applicable standards and specifications referenced in the tender.  

3. The Supplier shall provide necessary assistance, including access to records, test reports, and relevant documentation, during inspection and verification.  

4. Any non-conformity observed during inspection shall be rectified by the Supplier at no additional cost to the Purchaser before dispatch.  

5. Final inspection and verification shall be carried out at the destination as per the Purchaser’s requirements.  

6. The Supplier shall ensure that all test certificates, material certifications, and inspection reports are made available before shipment.  

7. The Purchaser reserves the right to reject any material failing to meet the specified requirements, with costs borne by the Supplier.  

8. The Supplier shall not dispatch any material without prior inspection clearance, if specified in the tender.  

9. Third-party inspection, if required, shall be arranged by the Supplier at the Purchaser’s discretion.  

10. The Supplier shall comply with all additional inspection and verification requirements as stipulated in the tender schedule.
