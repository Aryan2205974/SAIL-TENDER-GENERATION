**SUBSECTION: TECHNICAL ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA**  

1. The tenderer must have experience in supplying **Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks** for **ladle applications** in steel plants, with documented proof of execution in **Government Organizations/PSUs/Public Limited Companies** within the last **seven (07) years**.  

2. The tenderer shall provide evidence of **successfully completed works** as follows:  
   - **Three similar works**, each valued at **Rs. 51,42,692/- (excluding GST)** or higher.  
   - **Two similar works**, each valued at **Rs. 64,28,365/- (excluding GST)** or higher.  
   - **One similar work**, valued at **Rs. 1,02,85,384/- (excluding GST)** or higher.  

3. **Similar works** shall be defined as:  
   - Supply or installation of **refractory bricks** for **steel ladles** in **integrated steel plants**.  

4. Documentary proof shall include:  
   - **Work orders** or **completion certificates** issued by the employer.  
   - **TDS certificates (Form 16A/26AS)** for financial verification.  

5. The tenderer must possess **valid PF registration** with the **EPFO**.  

6. Experience from **Joint Venture Companies** shall be accepted only if one partner is a **Government Organization/PSU**.  

7. **MSEs and Start-ups** recognized by **DPIIT** may avail **15% relaxation** in financial turnover criteria.  

8. **Self-attested copies** of all supporting documents must be submitted with the bid.  

9. Failure to meet any eligibility criteria shall result in **rejection of the tender**.  

10. **No deviations** from the specified technical requirements shall be permitted.
