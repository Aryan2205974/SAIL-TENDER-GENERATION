**DOCUMENT SUBMISSION REQUIREMENTS**  

1. The Tenderer shall submit self-attested copies of Work Orders and Completion Certificates issued by the concerned executing authority for similar works completed in Central/State Govt. Organizations/PSUs/Joint Ventures with Govt. participation or Public Limited Companies.  

2. For substantially completed ongoing works, documents must evidence at least 50% completion and one year of execution, with executed value supported by RA bills or equivalent payment proofs.  

3. Financial turnover proof for the last 3 years ending 31st March 2025 shall include audited Profit & Loss statements or CA-certified turnover certificates, exclusive of GST, bearing UDIN and CA membership number.  

4. MSEs and DPIIT-recognized Start-ups claiming 15% relaxation in turnover criteria must submit valid registration certificates.  

5. Supply credentials for similar materials (e.g., refractory bricks) shall include purchase orders, invoices, and delivery challans from Govt./PSU clients, clearly indicating quantities and values.  

6. RDSO approval certificates (where applicable) must be submitted for product-specific tenders, with proof of prior supplies to Govt./PSU entities.  

7. Pollutions Control Board certificates with current validity are mandatory for manufacturing units, unless exempted under Clause 4 of GeM Bid references.  

8. Joint Venture partners must submit partnership agreements and individual eligibility documents if bidding collectively.  

9. Incomplete or unauthenticated documents will render the bid non-responsive.  

10. Additional sector-specific documents, if any, shall be specified in the tender schedule.
