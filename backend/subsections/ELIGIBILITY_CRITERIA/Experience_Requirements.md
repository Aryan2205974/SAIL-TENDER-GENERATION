**SUBSECTION: EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS**  

1. The Tenderer shall have experience of successfully completing or substantially completing similar works in Central/State Government Organizations/PSUs/Joint Ventures (with at least one Central/State Government or PSU partner)/Public Limited Companies during the last seven (07) years preceding the tender issuance date, as per the following criteria:  
   a) Three similar completed works, each costing not less than Rs. 0.90 Lakhs (excluding GST), **OR**  
   b) Two similar completed works, each costing not less than Rs. 1.12 Lakhs (excluding GST), **OR**  
   c) One similar completed work costing not less than Rs. 1.79 Lakhs (excluding GST).  

2. "Similar works" shall be defined as:  
   a) Supply/manufacture of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for ladle applications in steel plants, **OR**  
   b) Contracts related to refractory operations in steel plants or refractory manufacturing units.  

3. Experience certificates must be from Central/State Government entities, PSUs, or Joint Ventures with Government/PSU participation.  

4. Self-attested copies of work orders, completion certificates, or purchase orders shall be submitted as documentary evidence.  

5. MSEs and DPIIT-recognized Start-ups may claim a 15% relaxation in financial turnover criteria but must meet all technical eligibility requirements.  

6. Works executed as a subcontractor or consortium member will not be considered unless the Tenderer was the lead entity.  

7. Incomplete or disputed works shall not be counted toward eligibility.  

8. Financial turnover and experience criteria must be exclusive of GST unless otherwise specified.  

9. Cross-referencing of documents for multiple tenders is prohibited unless explicitly permitted.  

10. The Tender Committee reserves the right to verify submitted documents with issuing authorities.
