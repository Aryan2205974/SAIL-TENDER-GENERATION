**Financial Eligibility**

1. The Tenderer shall possess an Average Annual Financial Turnover during the last 03 years ending 31st March 2025 of at least Rs. 0.67 Lakhs (excluding GST).  

2. A relaxation of 15% in Financial Turnover criteria is provided for MSEs eligible for preference and Start-ups recognized by DPIIT.  

3. The Tenderer must submit a turnover certificate or audited profit and loss account statement for the last 03 years, exclusive of GST.  

4. Financial documents issued by a Chartered Accountant must include a unique document identification number (UDIN) and membership number of the CA.  

5. The turnover certificate and P&L statement must be in the name of the participating tenderer.  

6. For MSEs and Start-ups, the minimum Average Annual Financial Turnover requirement shall be Rs. 0.57 Lakhs (excluding GST) after applying the 15% relaxation.  

7. The Tenderer must ensure that all financial documents are self-attested and submitted along with the Techno-Commercial Bid.  

8. Failure to provide the required financial documents or discrepancies in the submitted documents shall render the tenderer ineligible.  

9. The financial eligibility criteria shall be strictly adhered to, and no exceptions shall be made unless explicitly stated in the tender document.
