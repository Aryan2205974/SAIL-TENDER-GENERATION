**Disqualification Criteria**  

1. Any deviation from the tender specifications or scope of supply must be explicitly indicated in writing, listed separately under a sheet titled "Deviation(s) Suggested." Failure to comply will result in disqualification.  

2. Tenderers must declare in writing if any proprietor, partner, or director of their company has any relations with employees of SAIL Corporate Office or SAIL Directors. Non-disclosure or false declaration will lead to disqualification.  

3. Tenderers must be an authorized service/business/channel partner of M/s Enterasys for maintenance of equipment covered under AMC. Non-compliance with this criterion will disqualify the bidder.  

4. Tenderers must not be blacklisted by any Government or undertaking organization. Evidence of blacklisting will result in disqualification.  

5. Tenderers must be registered as a company in India. Failure to provide valid registration documents will disqualify the bidder.  

6. Tenderers must have a full-fledged in-house service center or technical support office in Delhi/NCR. Absence of such facilities will lead to disqualification.  

7. Tenderers must meet the financial eligibility criteria, including an average annual turnover of Rs. 66.21 Lakhs for the last three years ending 31st March 2025. Non-compliance will disqualify the bidder.  

8. Tenderers must submit audited financial documents, including profit and loss statements, certified by a Chartered Accountant with UDIN and membership number. Incomplete or invalid documents will result in disqualification.  

9. Tenderers must have successfully completed similar works as specified in the tender, with a minimum value of Rs. 88.27 Lakhs per project. Failure to provide proof of such experience will disqualify the bidder.  

10. Tenderers must submit all requisite documents, including work orders, completion certificates, and RA bills, as specified in the tender. Incomplete or non-compliant submissions will lead to disqualification.
