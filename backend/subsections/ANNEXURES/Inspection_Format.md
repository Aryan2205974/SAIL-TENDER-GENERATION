**SUBSECTION: INSPECTION FORMAT**  

1. The bidder shall submit a detailed inspection protocol for Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks, including methodology, equipment, and acceptance criteria.  

2. Inspection shall be conducted at the manufacturer’s premises prior to dispatch, with prior intimation to the purchaser.  

3. The bidder shall provide certified test reports from NABL-accredited laboratories for all specified physical and chemical properties.  

4. Random sampling shall be performed as per IS 1528 (Part 23) or equivalent international standards.  

5. The purchaser reserves the right to conduct third-party inspection at the bidder’s cost if results are non-compliant.  

6. Rejected material shall be replaced by the bidder without additional cost within the agreed delivery period.  

7. Final inspection at the purchaser’s site shall include dimensional checks and visual examination for defects.  

8. The bidder shall furnish a certificate of conformity for each consignment, signed by an authorized quality representative.  

9. Non-destructive testing (NDT), if required, shall be performed as per ASTM C1171 or equivalent.  

10. Dispute resolution in inspection shall be referred to an independent agency mutually agreed upon by both parties.
