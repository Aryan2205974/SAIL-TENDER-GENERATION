**ANNEXURES**  
**Compliance Format**  

1. The bidder shall strictly adhere to the format provided in Annexure-A for submitting technical compliance against each specification parameter. Non-compliance shall be summarily rejected.  

2. All deviations, if any, must be explicitly listed in Annexure-B with justification and supporting technical data. Unreported deviations will render the bid non-responsive.  

3. Annexure-C shall be duly filled with certified test reports from NABL-accredited laboratories for the offered Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks, covering all specified physicochemical properties.  

4. Manufacturer’s works certificate (Annexure-D) must be submitted, confirming the bidder’s capability to produce bricks meeting SAIL’s SMS-II Ladle application requirements.  

5. Annexure-E shall include a detailed quality assurance plan, outlining stage-wise inspection and testing protocols during production.  

6. Bidder must submit Annexure-F, providing documented evidence of successful past supplies of similar bricks to integrated steel plants of minimum specified capacity.  

7. Annexure-G requires a signed undertaking for compliance with SAIL’s packaging, marking, and transportation standards.  

8. Any subcontracting arrangements must be declared in Annexure-H, along with subcontractor’s technical credentials and prior approvals.  

9. Annexure-I shall contain the bidder’s proposed installation methodology, if applicable, for site services.  

10. Failure to submit any mandatory annexure in the prescribed format shall result in disqualification.
