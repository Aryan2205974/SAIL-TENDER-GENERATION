**Guaranteed Technical Particulars**  

1. The Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks shall conform to IS 1528 or equivalent international standards.  

2. The bricks shall exhibit a minimum alumina (Al₂O₃) content of 70% by weight.  

3. The magnesia (MgO) content shall not be less than 15% by weight.  

4. The apparent porosity shall not exceed 18% as per ASTM C20.  

5. Cold crushing strength (CCS) shall be a minimum of 50 MPa.  

6. The thermal shock resistance shall be demonstrated through a minimum of 20 cycles (heating to 1100°C followed by water quenching) without structural failure.  

7. The bricks shall have a bulk density of ≥ 2.90 g/cm³.  

8. Refractoriness under load (RUL) shall be ≥ 1650°C (0.2 MPa, 2-hour test).  

9. The dimensional tolerance shall be within ±1.5% of the specified size.  

10. The supplier shall provide a certificate of analysis from a NABL-accredited laboratory for each batch.
