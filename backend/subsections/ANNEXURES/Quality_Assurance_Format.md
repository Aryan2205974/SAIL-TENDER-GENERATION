**SUBSECTION: QUALITY ASSURANCE FORMAT**  

1. The bidder shall furnish a detailed Quality Assurance Plan (QAP) outlining procedures for raw material selection, manufacturing, and testing of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks.  

2. The QAP must include certified test reports from accredited laboratories confirming compliance with specified chemical composition and physical properties.  

3. The bidder shall provide a certificate of conformity from the manufacturer, validating that the bricks meet the required refractory standards.  

4. Batch-wise test certificates for parameters such as bulk density, apparent porosity, and cold crushing strength shall be submitted.  

5. The bidder shall ensure traceability of each consignment through proper labeling and documentation, including heat numbers or batch codes.  

6. Non-destructive testing (NDT) reports, if applicable, shall be furnished for critical dimensions and structural integrity.  

7. The bidder shall submit a defect liability statement, warranting replacement of defective materials detected during pre-dispatch inspection.  

8. Third-party inspection, if mandated, shall be conducted at the bidder’s cost, with prior approval of the inspecting agency.  

9. The bidder shall provide a declaration affirming adherence to ISO 9001 or equivalent quality management systems.  

10. Any deviations from specified quality parameters shall be reported with corrective action proposals.
