**Technical Data Sheet**  

1. The Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks shall conform to the following chemical composition (percentage by weight):  
   - Al₂O₃: To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - MgO: To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - SiO₂: To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - Fe₂O₃: To be specified in the tender schedule.  

2. The physical properties shall meet the following criteria:  
   - Bulk Density (g/cm³): To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - Apparent Porosity (%): To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - Cold Crushing Strength (MPa): To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - Refractoriness Under Load (RUL) (°C): To be specified in the tender schedule.  

3. The thermal properties shall include:  
   - Thermal Conductivity (W/m·K) at specified temperatures.  
   - Thermal Expansion Coefficient (/°C) within the operational range.  

4. The bricks shall exhibit high resistance to slag penetration and thermal shock.  

5. Dimensional tolerances shall comply with ISO 10060-1 or equivalent standards.  

6. The manufacturer shall provide certified test reports from an accredited laboratory.  

7. The bricks shall be free from cracks, laminations, and other defects affecting performance.  

8. Packaging shall ensure protection against moisture and mechanical damage during transit.  

9. Each consignment shall be accompanied by a detailed material safety data sheet (MSDS).  

10. Any deviations from the specified requirements shall be explicitly declared with justification.
