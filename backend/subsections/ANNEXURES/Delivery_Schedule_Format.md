**Delivery Schedule Format**  

1. The bidder shall furnish a detailed delivery schedule aligned with the project timeline, ensuring uninterrupted supply as per operational requirements.  

2. The delivery schedule must include milestones for dispatch, transit, and handover at the designated location within Bokaro Steel Plant premises.  

3. All deliveries shall be accompanied by necessary documentation, including material test certificates and packing lists, as per SAIL’s standard requirements.  

4. Partial deliveries, if permitted, must be explicitly approved in writing by the purchaser prior to execution.  

5. The bidder shall confirm compliance with SAIL’s logistics and handling protocols, including unloading and storage guidelines at the site.  

6. Any deviations from the proposed delivery schedule must be communicated immediately, along with a revised timeline and mitigation plan.  

7. The bidder shall ensure that transportation and packaging prevent damage, corrosion, or contamination of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks.  

8. Delivery timelines shall be binding upon contract award, with liquidated damages applicable for delays beyond permissible limits.  

9. The purchaser reserves the right to modify delivery requirements, subject to mutual agreement and written confirmation.  

10. Final delivery schedules will be incorporated into the purchase order, superseding all prior communications.
