**DECLARATION FORMAT**  

1. The bidder shall furnish a duly signed declaration confirming compliance with all technical specifications as per the tender document.  

2. The bidder must declare that the offered Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks meet the chemical and physical properties specified in Section II (Technical Requirements).  

3. A declaration shall be submitted stating that the manufacturing process adheres to ISO 9001 or equivalent quality standards.  

4. The bidder shall provide a declaration affirming that no deviations or substitutions are made without prior written approval from the purchaser.  

5. A statement confirming the availability of raw materials from approved sources shall be included.  

6. The bidder must declare that all test certificates provided are authentic and obtained from accredited laboratories.  

7. A declaration shall be furnished confirming that the bidder has not been blacklisted by any PSU or government entity.  

8. The bidder shall submit a declaration acknowledging that any false statement may lead to disqualification or termination of contract.  

9. A statement confirming readiness to provide additional samples for testing, if required, shall be included.  

10. The bidder must declare acceptance of all terms and conditions stipulated in the tender document without reservation.
