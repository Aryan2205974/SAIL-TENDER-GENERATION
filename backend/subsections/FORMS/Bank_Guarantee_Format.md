**BANK GUARANTEE FORMAT**  

1. The successful bidder shall furnish an unconditional Bank Guarantee (BG) from any Scheduled Commercial Bank in India, valid for the period specified in the tender schedule.  

2. The BG shall be issued in the prescribed format provided by SAIL and shall be strictly as per the terms and conditions of the tender.  

3. The BG amount shall be as specified in the tender schedule and shall remain valid beyond the completion of all contractual obligations, including the warranty period.  

4. The BG shall be irrevocable and payable on demand without protest or demur, and no request for its reduction or release shall be entertained before the completion of the contract.  

5. The BG shall explicitly state that it is governed by Indian laws and jurisdiction of courts at the location of Bokaro Steel Plant.  

6. Any amendments or extensions to the BG shall be submitted at least 15 days before its expiry, failing which SAIL may encash the BG.  

7. The BG shall not be conditional upon any other agreement or documentation outside the tender terms.  

8. In case of encashment, the bank shall make immediate payment without seeking any further consent from the bidder.  

9. The BG shall be returned only after successful completion of all contractual obligations, including warranty provisions.  

10. Non-submission or non-compliance with BG terms shall entitle SAIL to take appropriate actions as per tender conditions.
