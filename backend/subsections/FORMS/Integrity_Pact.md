**INTEGRITY PACT**  

1. The Bidder shall adhere to the Integrity Pact as per SAIL’s standard guidelines to ensure transparency and fairness in the procurement process.  

2. The Bidder and SAIL mutually agree to prohibit any form of corrupt practices, including bribery, collusion, or undue influence during the tender process.  

3. The Bidder shall disclose any conflict of interest, including prior or current relationships with SAIL officials, which may affect impartiality.  

4. Violation of the Integrity Pact shall result in disqualification, forfeiture of EMD, and potential blacklisting from future SAIL tenders.  

5. The Bidder shall submit a signed Integrity Pact document in the prescribed format along with the bid.  

6. SAIL reserves the right to verify the Bidder’s compliance with the Integrity Pact through independent audits or investigations.  

7. The Bidder shall cooperate fully with any inquiries related to the Integrity Pact and provide necessary documentation upon request.  

8. Any attempt to circumvent the Integrity Pact terms shall be treated as a breach of contract and may attract legal action.  

9. The Bidder shall ensure all subcontractors, agents, or representatives involved in the contract also comply with the Integrity Pact.  

10. Disputes arising under the Integrity Pact shall be resolved as per the dispute resolution mechanism specified in the tender.
