**PRICE BID FORMAT**  

1. The Price Bid shall be submitted strictly as per the format provided in Annexure-X.  

2. All prices shall be quoted in Indian Rupees (INR), exclusive of GST, with applicable tax rates clearly indicated.  

3. The quoted price shall include all costs related to manufacturing, testing, packing, forwarding, and transportation up to the designated delivery point.  

4. Any deviations from the specified technical requirements must be explicitly listed with corresponding price adjustments.  

5. The bidder shall provide a detailed breakdown of unit rates, including raw material costs, processing charges, and overheads.  

6. Prices shall remain firm and fixed for the entire duration of the contract, with no escalation permitted unless specified otherwise in the tender schedule.  

7. Discounts, if any, shall be clearly indicated separately and will be considered in bid evaluation.  

8. The bidder shall confirm acceptance of the payment terms as stipulated in the tender document.  

9. Any additional charges not explicitly mentioned in the bid shall not be claimable at a later stage.  

10. Failure to adhere to the prescribed format may result in disqualification.
