**Bid Submission Form**  

1. The bidder shall submit the bid in the prescribed format, duly signed and stamped by an authorized representative.  

2. Bids must be submitted in sealed envelopes, clearly marked with the tender reference number and "Bid for Procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle Application."  

3. Bidders shall furnish all required technical and commercial documents as per the tender schedule.  

4. Any corrections or modifications in the bid must be authenticated by the bidder’s authorized signatory.  

5. Bids received after the stipulated deadline shall be summarily rejected.  

6. The bidder shall ensure that all pages of the bid document are sequentially numbered and bound securely.  

7. Electronic submission, if permitted, shall comply with the specified format and encryption standards.  

8. Bidders shall acknowledge all amendments, if any, issued by the purchaser prior to bid submission.  

9. Unsigned or incomplete bids shall be treated as non-responsive.  

10. The bidder shall confirm adherence to all terms and conditions stipulated in the tender documents.
