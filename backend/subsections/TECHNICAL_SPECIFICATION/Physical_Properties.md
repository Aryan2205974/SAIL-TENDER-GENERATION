**SUBSECTION: PHYSICAL PROPERTIES**  

1. The Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks shall conform to the following physical properties:  
   - Al₂O₃ content: Minimum 85%  
   - MgO content: Minimum 10%  
   - Bulk Density: ≥ 3.00 g/cm³  
   - Apparent Porosity: ≤ 18%  
   - Cold Crushing Strength: ≥ 50 MPa  

2. The bricks shall exhibit a thermal shock resistance of ≥ 20 cycles (heating to 1100°C followed by water quenching) without visible cracks or spalling.  

3. Refractoriness Under Load (RUL) shall be ≥ 1700°C (0.2 MPa load, 2-hour test duration).  

4. Permanent Linear Change (PLC) after reheating at 1600°C for 5 hours shall not exceed ±0.5%.  

5. The bricks shall be free from structural defects such as cracks, laminations, or voids affecting performance.  

6. Dimensional tolerances shall adhere to ISO 10060-1 standards, with maximum deviation of ±1% in length, width, and thickness.  

7. The bricks shall be supplied with smooth, even surfaces and sharp edges, free from excessive surface roughness or chipping.  

8. Chemical composition and physical properties shall be certified through independent laboratory testing, with test reports submitted prior to dispatch.  

9. Packaging shall ensure protection against moisture and mechanical damage during transit.  

10. Any deviation from specified properties must be explicitly declared and approved in writing prior to supply.
