**SUBSECTION: ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA**  

1. The Bidder shall strictly adhere to the technical specifications provided in this tender. Any deviation, unless explicitly approved in writing by the Purchaser, shall render the bid non-responsive.  

2. The offered Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks must conform to the chemical, physical, and dimensional properties as specified in the technical schedule. Non-compliance shall result in rejection of the consignment.  

3. The Bidder shall furnish a certificate of analysis from a NABL-accredited laboratory for each batch, confirming compliance with the specified parameters.  

4. The Purchaser reserves the right to conduct independent testing through an empaneled third-party laboratory. The cost of such testing, if the material fails to meet specifications, shall be borne by the Bidder.  

5. Material not meeting the acceptance criteria shall be replaced by the Bidder at no additional cost within the stipulated delivery period.  

6. The Bidder shall ensure that all supplied bricks are free from defects, including cracks, laminations, or uneven dimensions, as per industry standards.  

7. The Purchaser may conduct pre-dispatch inspection at the Bidder’s works. Non-compliance observed during inspection shall lead to rejection of the entire lot.  

8. The Bidder shall submit a detailed quality assurance plan (QAP) along with the bid, outlining manufacturing, testing, and inspection protocols.  

9. Final acceptance shall be subject to satisfactory performance during trial usage at the Purchaser’s facility.  

10. Any false declaration or non-compliance shall result in termination of the contract and legal action as per applicable laws.
