**SUBSECTION: CHEMICAL COMPOSITION**

1. The Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks shall conform to the following chemical composition:  
   - Alumina (Al₂O₃): To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - Magnesia (MgO): To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - Spinel (MgAl₂O₄): To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - Silica (SiO₂): To be specified in the tender schedule.  
   - Iron Oxide (Fe₂O₃): To be specified in the tender schedule.  

2. The chemical composition shall be verified through certified laboratory testing as per relevant Indian or international standards.  

3. The bidder shall provide a detailed chemical analysis report for each batch of bricks supplied, certified by an accredited laboratory.  

4. Any deviation from the specified chemical composition shall be grounds for rejection of the consignment.  

5. The bricks shall be free from impurities and contaminants that may adversely affect their performance in ladle applications.  

6. The bidder shall ensure uniformity in chemical composition across all bricks supplied under this contract.  

7. The chemical composition shall be maintained within the permissible tolerance limits as specified in the tender schedule.  

8. The bidder shall submit a certificate of compliance for the chemical composition along with each consignment.  

9. The bricks shall be manufactured using raw materials of consistent quality to ensure adherence to the specified chemical composition.  

10. The bidder shall provide a detailed methodology for maintaining and verifying the chemical composition during the manufacturing process.
