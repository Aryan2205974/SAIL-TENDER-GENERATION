**Personal Protective Equipment**  

1. The Contractor shall ensure that all personnel engaged in the execution of the work are provided with appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) as per applicable statutory regulations and industry standards.  

2. PPE shall include, but not be limited to, safety helmets, safety shoes, gloves, goggles, ear protection, and high-visibility clothing.  

3. The Contractor shall ensure that PPE is of approved quality, conforming to relevant Indian Standards or equivalent international standards.  

4. All PPE shall be inspected regularly for defects or damage, and any faulty equipment shall be replaced immediately.  

5. The Contractor shall provide training to all personnel on the correct use, maintenance, and limitations of the provided PPE.  

6. Adequate quantities of PPE shall be maintained at the worksite to ensure uninterrupted availability for all workers.  

7. The Contractor shall ensure that PPE is worn at all times during the execution of work, as per site safety requirements.  

8. The Contractor shall maintain records of PPE issuance, inspection, and training, which shall be made available for verification upon request.  

9. Non-compliance with PPE requirements shall result in immediate corrective action, including suspension of work if necessary.  

10. The Contractor shall bear all costs associated with the provision, maintenance, and replacement of PPE.
