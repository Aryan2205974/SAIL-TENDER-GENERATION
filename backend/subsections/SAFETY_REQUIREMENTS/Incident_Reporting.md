**Incident Reporting**  

1. The Contractor shall immediately report any safety incident, including near-misses, injuries, or equipment damage, occurring during the execution of the contract to the Bokaro Steel Plant Safety Department.  

2. Incident reports must be submitted in writing within 24 hours, detailing the nature of the incident, location, personnel involved, and immediate corrective actions taken.  

3. The Contractor shall cooperate fully with SAIL’s investigation team and provide all necessary records, witness statements, and site access for incident analysis.  

4. No alteration or removal of evidence from the incident site shall be permitted without prior authorization from the Bokaro Steel Plant Safety Department.  

5. The Contractor shall implement corrective and preventive measures as directed by SAIL within the stipulated timeframe.  

6. Failure to report incidents promptly or obstruction of investigations shall be treated as a breach of contract and may result in penalties.  

7. All incident reports shall be maintained by the Contractor for a minimum period of three years and made available for audit upon request.  

8. The Contractor shall conduct mandatory safety briefings for all personnel following an incident to reinforce preventive measures.  

9. SAIL reserves the right to impose additional safety protocols based on incident findings.  

10. Repeated incidents due to non-compliance may lead to termination of the contract.
