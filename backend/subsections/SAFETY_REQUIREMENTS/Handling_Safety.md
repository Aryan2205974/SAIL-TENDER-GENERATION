**5. Handling Safety**  

5.1 The bidder shall ensure safe handling procedures for Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks during loading, transportation, and unloading to prevent damage or degradation.  

5.2 Proper lifting equipment, including cranes or forklifts with adequate load-bearing capacity, shall be used for handling bricks to avoid breakage.  

5.3 Bricks shall be securely packed in moisture-resistant, shock-absorbent packaging to prevent exposure to adverse weather conditions during transit.  

5.4 Stacking height during storage and transit shall not exceed manufacturer-recommended limits to prevent structural instability.  

5.5 Personnel involved in handling shall wear appropriate PPE, including gloves, safety shoes, and helmets, as per industrial safety standards.  

5.6 Hazardous manual handling shall be minimized through mechanized lifting aids where feasible.  

5.7 The bidder shall provide handling instructions, including weight distribution and stacking guidelines, to prevent accidents.  

5.8 Damaged or improperly stored bricks shall be segregated and reported immediately to avoid unsafe usage.  

5.9 The bidder shall comply with applicable statutory safety regulations for material handling.  

5.10 Any spillage or breakage during handling shall be promptly cleaned to prevent workplace hazards.
