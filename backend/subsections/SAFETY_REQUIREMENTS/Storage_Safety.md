**SUBSECTION: STORAGE SAFETY**  

1. The Contractor shall ensure proper storage of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks in a dry, well-ventilated area, free from moisture and contaminants.  

2. Stacking height shall not exceed manufacturer-recommended limits to prevent structural damage or instability.  

3. Storage facilities must maintain ambient temperature conditions as specified in the technical datasheet.  

4. Bricks shall be stored on pallets or raised platforms to avoid direct contact with the ground.  

5. The storage area shall be segregated from hazardous materials, chemicals, or incompatible substances.  

6. Fire prevention measures, including no-smoking zones and fire extinguishers, shall be enforced within the storage vicinity.  

7. Handling equipment (e.g., forklifts, cranes) shall be operated by trained personnel to prevent accidental damage.  

8. The Contractor shall ensure proper labeling and segregation of different brick grades/lots to avoid mix-ups.  

9. Periodic inspection of stored bricks shall be conducted to identify any deterioration or damage.  

10. Emergency spillage or breakage protocols shall be established and communicated to all personnel.
