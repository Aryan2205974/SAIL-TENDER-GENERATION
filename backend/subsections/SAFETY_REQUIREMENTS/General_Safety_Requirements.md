**General Safety Requirements**

1. The supplier shall ensure that all materials supplied comply with applicable safety standards and regulations, including but not limited to IS, ISO, and other relevant industrial safety norms.

2. All personnel involved in the manufacturing, handling, and transportation of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks must be adequately trained in safety protocols and equipped with appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE).

3. The supplier shall provide Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) for the bricks, detailing handling, storage, and emergency response measures.

4. The supplier shall ensure that all packaging and labeling of the bricks conform to safety standards, including clear markings for hazardous materials, if applicable.

5. The supplier shall conduct a risk assessment for the entire supply chain process and submit a safety management plan addressing identified risks.

6. The supplier shall ensure that all equipment used in the production and handling of the bricks is regularly inspected and maintained to prevent safety hazards.

7. The supplier shall provide a safety audit report from a recognized authority certifying compliance with safety standards in their manufacturing facility.

8. The supplier shall ensure that transportation of the bricks adheres to safety regulations, including proper securing of loads and compliance with road safety norms.

9. The supplier shall promptly report any safety incidents or near-misses during the execution of the contract and take corrective actions to prevent recurrence.

10. The supplier shall cooperate with Bokaro Steel Plant’s safety team during site visits or inspections to ensure compliance with safety requirements.
