**Transportation Safety**  

1. The bidder shall ensure safe transportation of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks to Bokaro Steel Plant, adhering to applicable road safety regulations.  

2. All vehicles used for transportation must be adequately covered and secured to prevent damage from weather or handling.  

3. The bidder shall comply with statutory requirements for hazardous material transport, if applicable, as per Indian Motor Vehicle Rules.  

4. Proper stacking and cushioning materials shall be used to prevent breakage or abrasion during transit.  

5. The bidder shall furnish a transportation plan detailing route optimization, handling protocols, and emergency response measures.  

6. Any incidents during transit, including delays or damages, shall be reported immediately to Bokaro Steel Plant authorities.  

7. The bidder shall ensure drivers and handlers are trained in safe loading, unloading, and transit procedures.  

8. Transport documentation, including waybills and inspection certificates, shall accompany each consignment.  

9. The bidder shall bear liability for any transportation-related non-compliance leading to material rejection.  

10. Final delivery shall be verified against packing lists and quality checks before acceptance.
