**SUBSECTION: CONTRACT FORMATION**  

1. The contract shall be formed upon acceptance of the successful bidder’s offer by SAIL, subject to compliance with all tender terms and conditions.  

2. The contract duration shall be as specified in the tender schedule, unless otherwise amended by mutual agreement in writing.  

3. The tender submission shall consist of three parts:  
   - Part I: EMD Bid (including EMD or exemption documents).  
   - Part II: Techno-Commercial Bid (excluding price).  
   - Part III: Price Bid (as per prescribed format).  

4. The General Conditions of Contract for Procurement of Services (SAIL-S1) shall apply, available on SAIL’s tender portal (www.sailtenders.co.in).  

5. Bidders are advised to visit the VISL site to fully understand the scope of work before submission. Clarifications may be sought from the Contract Executing Authority.  

6. In case of conflict between General and Special Conditions of Contract, the Special Conditions shall prevail.  

7. Tenders must be submitted as per the prescribed mode and addressed to the designated office, failing which they shall be rejected.  

8. Any modifications to the contract shall be valid only if issued in writing and duly authorized by SAIL.  

9. The contract shall be governed by the laws of India, with jurisdiction at the location of the issuing authority.  

10. SAIL reserves the right to cancel or amend the tender process at any stage without assigning reasons.
