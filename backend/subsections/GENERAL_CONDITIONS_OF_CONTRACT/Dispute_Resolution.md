**Dispute Resolution**  

5.31 In the event of any dispute, difference, or disagreement arising out of or in connection with this contract, the parties shall make every effort to resolve the same amicably through mutual consultation.  

5.32 If the dispute cannot be resolved amicably within 30 (thirty) days from the date of written notice by either party, the matter shall be referred to the Contract Executing Authority for resolution.  

5.33 The Contract Executing Authority shall, after reviewing the matter, provide a written decision within 30 (thirty) days of receiving the dispute.  

5.34 If the dispute remains unresolved after the decision of the Contract Executing Authority, either party may refer the matter to arbitration in accordance with the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996, as amended from time to time.  

5.35 The arbitration shall be conducted by a sole arbitrator appointed mutually by both parties. In case of disagreement on the appointment, the arbitrator shall be appointed by the Chairman & Managing Director of SAIL.  

5.36 The arbitration proceedings shall be conducted in English, and the venue of arbitration shall be Bokaro, Jharkhand, India.  

5.37 The decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding on both parties.  

5.38 During the pendency of any dispute, the parties shall continue to perform their respective obligations under the contract, unless otherwise directed by the arbitrator.  

5.39 The costs of arbitration, including fees and expenses, shall be borne as determined by the arbitrator.  

5.40 Nothing in this clause shall preclude either party from seeking interim relief from a court of competent jurisdiction.
