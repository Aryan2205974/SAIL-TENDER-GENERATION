**PAYMENT TERMS**  

**Recovery Provisions**  

1. The Contractor shall be liable for recovery of any excess payment made due to errors in billing, measurement, or pricing discrepancies.  

2. Recovery shall be effected from subsequent bills or through a separate demand notice if no further payments are due.  

3. In case of non-compliance with contractual specifications, recovery of costs for rectification or replacement shall be made from the Contractor.  

4. Any advance payment shall be adjusted proportionately against subsequent invoices as per the agreed repayment schedule.  

5. If the Contractor fails to meet delivery or quality obligations, SAIL reserves the right to recover liquidated damages as per the contract terms.  

6. Unauthorized deviations from approved drawings or materials shall result in recovery of additional costs incurred for correction.  

7. Overpayments detected during audit or reconciliation shall be recovered with applicable interest as per prevailing bank rates.  

8. Recovery of statutory dues, if not deducted at source, shall be made from the Contractor’s dues.  

9. Any penalties imposed due to non-performance shall be recoverable from the Contractor’s pending payments or security deposits.  

10. Disputes regarding recovery shall be resolved as per the arbitration clause of the contract.
