**SUBSECTION: INVOICE REQUIREMENTS**  

1. The Supplier shall submit original invoices in triplicate, clearly marked "Original," "Duplicate," and "Triplicate."  

2. Invoices must include the Purchase Order number, item description, quantity, unit price, total value, and applicable taxes.  

3. GST details, including HSN code and tax breakdown, shall be explicitly stated on the invoice.  

4. Invoices shall bear the Supplier’s authorized signatory with name, designation, and company stamp.  

5. Separate invoices must be issued for each consignment, referencing the corresponding Delivery Challan number.  

6. Invoices shall be submitted within seven days of dispatch, accompanied by proof of delivery.  

7. Any discrepancies in invoiced quantities or pricing shall be rectified through a supplementary debit/credit note.  

8. Electronic invoices (e-invoices) must comply with the GSTN portal requirements and include the IRN and QR code.  

9. Failure to comply with invoice requirements may result in delayed payments.  

10. To be specified in the tender schedule.
