**5. Supporting Documents**  

5.1 The Bidder shall submit the following documents along with the commercial bid:  

5.1.1 Copy of valid Quality Assurance Certificate (QAC) for Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks from a recognized authority.  

5.1.2 Detailed technical specifications and test certificates confirming compliance with SAIL’s prescribed standards.  

5.1.3 Manufacturer’s Authorisation Letter (if bidder is not the manufacturer).  

5.1.4 Undertaking for supply of genuine material as per approved specifications.  

5.2 The following documents shall be submitted upon contract award:  

5.2.1 Original performance guarantee in the prescribed format.  

5.2.2 Inspection and testing protocol from an accredited laboratory.  

5.2.3 Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) for the supplied bricks.  

5.3 Any additional documents as specified in the tender schedule shall be furnished within the stipulated time.  

5.4 Failure to submit requisite documents may result in bid rejection or forfeiture of security.  

5.5 All documents must be duly signed, stamped, and submitted in the prescribed format.  

5.6 Unauthenticated or incomplete submissions shall be treated as non-responsive.  

5.7 Bidders shall ensure documents are free from discrepancies; corrections require counter-signing.  

5.8 Electronic submission, if permitted, shall follow SAIL’s specified guidelines.  

5.9 To be specified in the tender schedule.
