**PAYMENT TERMS**  

**SUBSECTION: PAYMENT CONDITIONS**  

1. Payment shall be made through Letter of Credit (LC) or Bank Guarantee (BG) as per SAIL’s standard payment terms.  

2. The successful bidder shall submit an invoice along with supporting documents, including dispatch details and inspection reports, for processing payment.  

3. Payment shall be released within 30 days from the date of receipt of complete documents, subject to verification.  

4. No advance payment shall be made unless explicitly specified in the tender schedule.  

5. In case of delayed payment beyond the stipulated period, interest as per applicable RBI guidelines shall be payable.  

6. The bidder shall bear all banking charges related to LC/BG issuance, except those explicitly borne by SAIL as per tender terms.  

7. Any discrepancies in invoices or supporting documents shall result in delayed processing until rectification.  

8. Final payment shall be subject to satisfactory performance and compliance with contract terms.  

9. Payment disputes, if any, shall be resolved as per the arbitration clause of the contract.  

10. Taxes, duties, and statutory levies shall be paid as per applicable laws and shall not be included in the quoted price unless specified.
