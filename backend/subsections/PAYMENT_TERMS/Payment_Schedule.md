**PAYMENT SCHEDULE**  

1. Payment shall be released against submission of invoices along with supporting documents as per the terms specified in the tender schedule.  

2. Advance payment, if applicable, shall be made upon submission of an unconditional Bank Guarantee of equivalent value, valid as per the tender terms.  

3. Interim payments shall be made based on the percentage of work completed or materials supplied, subject to verification and certification by the designated authority.  

4. Final payment shall be released only after successful completion of supply, inspection, and acceptance of materials as per the tender specifications.  

5. Payments shall be made within the stipulated time frame from the date of receipt of complete and valid documents, subject to compliance with tender conditions.  

6. Any deductions, such as taxes, levies, or penalties, shall be made as per applicable laws and tender provisions.  

7. In case of delay in payment beyond the agreed period, interest as per the tender terms shall be payable by the purchaser.  

8. Payment shall be made through electronic transfer to the supplier’s designated bank account, details of which must be provided in advance.  

9. Disputes, if any, regarding payments shall be resolved as per the dispute resolution mechanism outlined in the tender document.  

10. Any other payment-related terms shall be as specified in the tender schedule.
