**SUBSECTION: QUALITY REQUIREMENTS**  

1. The tenderer shall supply Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks conforming to the technical specifications as stipulated in the tender schedule.  

2. The bricks must be suitable for SMS-II Ladle application at Bokaro Steel Plant, ensuring high thermal shock resistance, corrosion resistance, and structural integrity under operational conditions.  

3. The bidder shall submit certified test reports from a NABL-accredited laboratory for the following parameters:  
   a) Chemical composition (Al₂O₃, MgO, SiO₂, Fe₂O₃)  
   b) Physical properties (Bulk density, Apparent porosity, Cold crushing strength)  
   c) Refractoriness under load (RUL) and thermal expansion characteristics.  

4. Raw materials used in manufacturing shall comply with international standards (ISO/BS/ASTM) or equivalent, with traceability certificates for each batch.  

5. The supplier shall provide a detailed quality control plan, including in-process inspection protocols and final product testing methodology.  

6. Samples may be subjected to third-party verification at SAIL-nominated laboratories at the bidder’s cost, if deemed necessary.  

7. Non-conforming materials shall be replaced at no additional cost to the purchaser, including transportation and handling charges.  

8. The bidder must furnish a warranty against manufacturing defects for a period to be specified in the tender schedule.  

9. Packing shall ensure protection against moisture and mechanical damage, with clear marking of batch numbers and manufacturing dates.  

10. To be specified in the tender schedule.
