**SUBSECTION: MANUFACTURING REQUIREMENTS**  

1. The tenderer must have experience in the supply of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for steel plant applications, specifically for ladle lining in SMS-II operations.  

2. The bidder shall provide proof of having executed:  
   a) Two similar completed works each costing not less than Rs. 39.22 Lakhs.  
   b) One similar completed work costing not less than Rs. 62.75 Lakhs.  

3. Similar work shall be defined as:  
   a) Supply of refractory bricks or monolithic materials for steel/refractory plants.  
   b) Experience in production contracts involving MCB/AMC bricks or related refractory items.  

4. The bidder shall submit self-attested documents, including:  
   a) Copies of relevant work orders.  
   b) Valid labour licence certificates (if applicable).  
   c) Work completion certificates issued by the concerned organizations.  

5. The contractor shall ensure compliance with raw material specifications, including finer fractions supplied by the purchaser, if applicable.  

6. The management may provide support such as:  
   a) Raw materials, additives, and packing materials.  
   b) Equipment including mixers, forklifts, and cranes for material handling.  

7. The tenderer shall furnish daily production reports in pre-printed pro-forma, with one copy submitted to the operating authority.  

8. All manufacturing processes shall adhere to industry standards and purchaser-approved quality parameters.  

9. The bidder must ensure uninterrupted production capacity to meet the specified delivery schedule.  

10. Any deviations from the prescribed manufacturing process must be approved in writing by the purchaser.
