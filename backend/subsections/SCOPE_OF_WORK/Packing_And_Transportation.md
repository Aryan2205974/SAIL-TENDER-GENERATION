**Packing And Transportation**

1. The materials shall be packed in 1 tonne HDPE bags (Jumbo bags) with double stitch, stitched closed and sealed properly to avoid losing or tampering. The bags shall have bottom discharge and shall be provided with strengthened hooks/handles to lift the bag by crane at a height of 25-30 meters for unloading in bunkers.  

2. Each bag shall be marked/stamped in indelible ink in a contrasting color with the name of the supplier, item name, and net weight of the material. A unique identification number shall be written on each bag of the same consignment.  

3. The tenderer shall ensure that the packing materials, including 25/50 kgs/Jumbo outer and inner bags, are provided by the employer for packing the masses as specified.  

4. The tenderer shall arrange for the transportation of the materials to the designated location at Bokaro Steel Plant, ensuring compliance with all applicable transportation regulations.  

5. The tenderer shall not be entitled to any payment for supplied quantities exceeding (+) 15 MT tolerance limit or (+) 5% of the ordered quantity, whichever is less.  

6. The tenderer shall ensure that all transportation and handling equipment, including cranes, hoists, and forklifts, are operated safely and efficiently during the unloading process.  

7. The tenderer shall provide pre-printed pro-forma for daily production reports, with the first copy submitted to the Operating Authority/Executing Officer along with the bill.  

8. The tenderer shall ensure that all packing and transportation activities are carried out in accordance with the specifications and requirements outlined in the tender documents.  

9. Any damages or losses incurred during transportation due to improper packing or handling shall be the sole responsibility of the tenderer.
