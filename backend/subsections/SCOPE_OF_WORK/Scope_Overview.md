**SCOPE OF WORK**  

**3.1 Scope Overview**  

1. The contractor shall supply Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS-II Ladle application at Bokaro Steel Plant, adhering to technical specifications and quality standards as stipulated in the tender schedule.  

2. The scope includes manufacturing, testing, packing, and timely delivery of refractory bricks as per SAIL’s approved design and material requirements.  

3. The contractor must ensure compliance with production processes, including crushing, grinding, mixing, pressing, and quality checks, as per industry best practices.  

4. Raw materials, additives, and finer fractions required for production shall be arranged by the contractor unless otherwise specified in the tender schedule.  

5. The contractor shall provide necessary manpower, equipment, and supervision for production, ensuring uninterrupted operations as per SAIL’s requirements.  

6. SRCL reserves the right to exercise quantity variation clauses, with permissible deviations as specified in the tender schedule.  

7. The contractor shall submit daily production reports in pre-printed proforma, duly certified by the executing authority, for verification and billing purposes.  

8. All packing materials, including jumbo bags (25/50 kg), shall be arranged by the contractor unless otherwise stated.  

9. The contractor shall adhere to environmental and safety regulations, including valid licenses and certifications, as applicable.  

10. Any additional support, such as utilities (electricity, water, compressed air) or machinery (mixers, forklifts), shall be as per mutual agreement and tender terms.
