**SUBSECTION: DELIVERY FAILURE**  

1. **Penalty for Non-Delivery**: If the contractor fails to deliver the stipulated quantity of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks as per the agreed schedule, a penalty of Rs. 5000/- per day shall be levied for each day of delay attributable to the contractor.  

2. **Exclusions**: No penalty shall be imposed for delays caused by force majeure events, non-availability of raw materials, or major power failures, provided such instances are duly certified by the Bokaro Steel Plant authority.  

3. **Authority Decision**: The imposition and waiver of penalties shall be at the sole discretion of the Engineer-in-Charge (EIC) or Officer-in-Charge (OIC), whose decision shall be final and binding.  

4. **Partial Delivery Shortfall**: If the contractor delivers less than the minimum required quantity per day, a proportional penalty shall apply based on the deficit, subject to the EIC/OIC’s approval.  

5. **Repeated Default**: In case of recurring delivery failures, the Bokaro Steel Plant reserves the right to terminate the contract after issuing a written notice, without prejudice to other remedies.  

6. **Documentation**: The contractor must submit a written explanation for any delivery failure within 48 hours, failing which the penalty shall be automatically enforced.  

7. **Penalty Recovery**: All penalties shall be deducted from the contractor’s running bills or security deposit, as deemed fit by the EIC/OIC.  

8. **Grace Period**: No penalty shall be levied for delays up to 2 days, provided the contractor intimates the EIC/OIC in advance with valid reasons.  

9. **Dispute Resolution**: Any dispute regarding penalties shall be resolved as per the arbitration clause of the contract.  

10. **Finality**: The EIC/OIC’s determination on delivery failures and penalties shall be conclusive.
