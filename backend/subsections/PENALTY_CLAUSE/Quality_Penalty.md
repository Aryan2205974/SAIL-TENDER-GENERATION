**SUBSECTION: QUALITY PENALTY**  

1. If the supplied Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks fail to meet the specified technical parameters as per the tender requirements, a penalty of Rs. 5,000/- per occasion shall be imposed. The decision of the EO/OA shall be final.  

2. In case of deviation from the approved formulation or manufacturing process, a penalty of Rs. 1,000/- per occasion shall be levied. The Shift In-charge or EO’s observation shall be binding.  

3. If the bricks exhibit defects such as cracks, dimensional inaccuracies, or improper sintering, a penalty of Rs. 2,000/- per batch shall be applicable.  

4. Non-compliance with quality test results as per agreed standards shall attract a penalty of Rs. 3,000/- per test failure.  

5. Repeated quality failures beyond three instances may lead to termination of the contract at the discretion of the EO/OA.  

6. Penalty for delayed submission of mandatory test certificates shall be Rs. 500/- per day of delay.  

7. If rejected bricks exceed 5% of the supplied quantity, a penalty of Rs. 1,000/- per additional 1% rejection shall apply.  

8. The contractor shall rectify quality defects at their own cost within seven days of notification, failing which a penalty of Rs. 2,000/- per day shall be imposed.  

9. All penalties shall be deducted from the contractor’s running bills or security deposit.  

10. Disputes, if any, shall be resolved as per the decision of the EO/OA.
