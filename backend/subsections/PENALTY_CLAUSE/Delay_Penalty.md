**DELAY PENALTY**  

1. A penalty of Rs. 1000/- shall be levied for each hour or part thereof of delay in job initiation.  

2. A penalty of Rs. 1000/- shall be levied for each hour or part thereof of delay beyond the stipulated job completion timeframe.  

3. In case of unavailability of a pickup van for more than one hour without prior information, no hiring charges shall be paid for the duration of unavailability. Additionally, a penalty equal to three times the agreed hourly hire charges of the pickup van, including driver, shall be recovered from the contractor.  

4. If a pickup van is found without proper and valid documents or with a defective odometer, no hiring charges shall be paid for the day. A penalty equal to three times the agreed daily hire charges shall also be recovered from the contractor.  

5. For deployment of manpower less than the required number, a penalty shall be calculated as follows: Penalty (Rs.) = (Required Manpower - Deployed Manpower) X Wage Rate for the applicable skill level X 0.25.  

6. In exceptional circumstances, where manpower with lower skill levels is deployed (with approval from the HOD), payment shall be made based on the actual skill level deployed.  

7. Failure to meet the minimum production target of 32 MT/day, attributable to the contractor, shall incur a penalty of Rs. 5000/- per day.  

8. If production in the Eirich Mixer falls below 30 MT/day primarily due to non-supply of personnel, a penalty of Rs. 2000/- per day shall be levied.  

9. Deviation from the prescribed formulation of batches for bricks and monolithic items, as observed by the Shift I/C or EO, shall attract a penalty of Rs. 1000/- per occasion.  

10. All penalties shall be final as per the decision of the EO/OA.
