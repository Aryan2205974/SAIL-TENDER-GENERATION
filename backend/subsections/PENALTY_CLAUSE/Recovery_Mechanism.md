**6. Recovery Mechanism**  

6.1 In case of failure to meet the specified quality parameters or delivery timelines, the supplier shall be liable for penalties as per the provisions of this tender.  

6.2 The penalty shall be recovered proportionately from the contract value based on the extent of non-compliance.  

6.3 Recovery shall be effected through deduction from running bills or any other payments due to the supplier.  

6.4 If the supplier fails to rectify defects within the stipulated time, Bokaro Steel Plant reserves the right to impose additional penalties.  

6.5 No penalty shall be imposed if the delay or non-conformance is due to force majeure conditions, subject to prior written approval.  

6.6 The recovery process shall be initiated only after issuing a written notice to the supplier, allowing a reasonable opportunity for clarification.  

6.7 Disputes, if any, regarding penalty imposition shall be resolved as per the arbitration clause of this tender.  

6.8 Unresolved penalty disputes may lead to termination of the contract at the discretion of Bokaro Steel Plant.  

6.9 All recoveries shall be adjusted against the Performance Bank Guarantee if payments are insufficient.  

6.10 The decision of Bokaro Steel Plant regarding penalty imposition and recovery shall be final and binding.
