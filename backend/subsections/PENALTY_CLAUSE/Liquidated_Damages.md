**SUBSECTION: Liquidated Damages**  

1. The Supplier shall be liable to pay liquidated damages for any delay in delivery beyond the stipulated schedule, as per the rates specified in the tender document.  

2. Liquidated damages shall be calculated as a percentage of the delayed portion of the contract value, deducted on a weekly basis, subject to a maximum cap as per tender terms.  

3. The deduction of liquidated damages shall not relieve the Supplier from any other contractual obligations, including timely completion of remaining deliveries.  

4. If the delay exceeds the maximum permissible period, the Purchaser reserves the right to terminate the contract without prejudice to other remedies available under law.  

5. The Supplier shall not be entitled to any extension of time unless a valid force majeure event is communicated and accepted by the Purchaser in writing.  

6. Any dispute regarding the imposition of liquidated damages shall be resolved as per the arbitration clause of the contract.  

7. The Purchaser may adjust liquidated damages from any payments due to the Supplier, including Performance Guarantee or Security Deposit, if applicable.  

8. The Supplier shall not claim exemption from liquidated damages on grounds of minor or partial delays.  

9. The rate and cap for liquidated damages shall be as specified in the commercial terms of the tender.  

10. The imposition of liquidated damages shall be without prejudice to any other rights or claims of the Purchaser under the contract.
