**Bid Submission Procedure**  

1. **Bid Format**: Bids shall be submitted in the prescribed format as detailed in the tender documents, including all requisite annexures and declarations.  

2. **Submission Mode**: Bids must be submitted electronically through the designated e-procurement portal of SAIL, with a valid digital signature. Physical submissions will not be accepted unless explicitly permitted.  

3. **Bid Validity**: Bids shall remain valid for a period as specified in the tender schedule. Any bid not meeting this requirement shall be rejected.  

4. **Modification/Withdrawal**: Bidders may modify or withdraw bids before the bid submission deadline. No changes shall be permitted thereafter.  

5. **Late Bids**: Bids received after the stipulated deadline shall be summarily rejected and returned unopened.  

6. **Documentation**: All supporting documents, including technical and commercial bids, shall be uploaded as per the prescribed file-naming conventions.  

7. **Acknowledgement**: An automated acknowledgement receipt will be generated upon successful submission, which the bidder must retain for reference.  

8. **Clarifications**: Queries regarding bid submission shall be addressed through the e-procurement portal’s query module only.  

9. **Completeness**: Incomplete bids or those lacking mandatory documents shall be deemed non-responsive.  

10. **Compliance**: Bidders must adhere to all instructions outlined in this section; non-compliance may result in disqualification.
