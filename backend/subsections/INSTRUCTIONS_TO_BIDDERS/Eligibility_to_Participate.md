**SUBSECTION: ELIGIBILITY TO PARTICIPATE**  

1. The bidder must be a manufacturer or authorized distributor of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks with documented proof of technical collaboration, if applicable.  

2. The bidder shall furnish a certificate of compliance with relevant international standards (ISO/EN/DIN) for the supplied material.  

3. The bidder must demonstrate prior supply of similar refractory materials to integrated steel plants or comparable heavy industries.  

4. The bidder shall provide a self-declaration confirming no involvement in corrupt or fraudulent practices, as per extant PSU guidelines.  

5. The bidder must submit a valid Quality Assurance Plan (QAP) covering manufacturing, testing, and dispatch protocols.  

6. The bidder shall possess adequate production capacity to meet the delivery schedule specified in the tender.  

7. The bidder must confirm adherence to environmental and safety regulations applicable to refractory manufacturing.  

8. The bidder shall submit proof of financial stability, including audited balance sheets for the last three years.  

9. Joint ventures or consortia must submit a legally binding agreement defining roles and liabilities.  

10. The bidder must acknowledge acceptance of SAIL’s general purchase conditions without deviation.
