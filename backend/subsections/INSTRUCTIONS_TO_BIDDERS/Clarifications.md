**Clarifications**  

1. Any clarifications sought by bidders regarding the tender document must be submitted in writing to the designated authority within the stipulated period mentioned in the tender schedule.  

2. Clarifications will be issued only in response to written queries. Verbal communications or informal requests will not be entertained.  

3. The responses to clarifications, if any, will be issued as an addendum/corrigendum to the tender document and will be binding on all bidders.  

4. Bidders are advised to regularly check the official tender portal for any updates, addenda, or corrigenda issued during the tender period.  

5. Failure to seek clarification on any ambiguous or unclear aspect of the tender document will not be considered as a valid ground for any claim or dispute at a later stage.  

6. Clarifications provided by the purchaser will form an integral part of the tender document and must be acknowledged by the bidder in their submission.  

7. Any attempt by bidders to influence the clarification process through unofficial channels will lead to disqualification.  

8. The purchaser reserves the right to modify or withdraw any clarification issued, if deemed necessary, with prior notification to all bidders.  

9. Clarifications sought after the deadline for queries will not be considered, and no further correspondence will be entertained.  

10. The purchaser’s decision on clarifications shall be final and binding on all bidders.
