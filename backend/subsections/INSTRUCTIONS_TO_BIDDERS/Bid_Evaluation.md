**SUBSECTION: BID EVALUATION**  

1. The bids shall be evaluated based on compliance with technical specifications, commercial terms, and other conditions stipulated in the tender document.  

2. Only bids meeting the minimum qualifying requirements shall be considered for further evaluation.  

3. The lowest evaluated bid (LEB) shall be determined after adjusting for any arithmetical errors, discounts, or deviations permissible under the tender terms.  

4. Bidders offering deviations not covered under permissible terms may be rejected at the sole discretion of SAIL.  

5. Negotiations, if required, shall be conducted only with the LEB bidder, and no further opportunity for revision shall be granted to other bidders.  

6. The decision of SAIL in bid evaluation shall be final and binding on all bidders.  

7. Bids received after the stipulated deadline shall be summarily rejected without any further consideration.  

8. SAIL reserves the right to accept or reject any bid, in whole or in part, without assigning any reason.  

9. The bid evaluation process shall be conducted as per SAIL’s internal guidelines and procurement policies.  

10. The final award shall be subject to the approval of competent authorities.
