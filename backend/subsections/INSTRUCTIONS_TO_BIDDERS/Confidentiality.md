**SUBSECTION: CONFIDENTIALITY**  

1. The tenderer shall maintain strict confidentiality of all information, documents, and data provided by SAIL Refractory Company Limited (SRCL) in connection with this tender.  

2. No part of the tender documents, specifications, or commercial terms shall be disclosed to any third party without prior written consent from SRCL.  

3. Unauthorized dissemination of tender-related information may result in disqualification and forfeiture of the Earnest Money Deposit (EMD).  

4. All proprietary data, including technical drawings and process details, shall be used solely for the purpose of bid preparation and execution of the contract, if awarded.  

5. The tenderer shall ensure that its employees, subcontractors, and agents adhere to the confidentiality obligations outlined herein.  

6. Breach of confidentiality may lead to legal action, including blacklisting from future tenders of SRCL and other SAIL units.  

7. Upon completion of the tender process, unsuccessful bidders shall return or destroy all confidential materials as directed by SRCL.  

8. The obligations under this clause shall survive the termination or expiry of the tender process.  

9. Any clarification sought regarding confidential aspects must be addressed exclusively through the designated tender authority.  

10. Non-compliance with confidentiality requirements shall render the bid non-responsive.
