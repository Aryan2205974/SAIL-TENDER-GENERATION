**SUBSECTION: BID SECURITY**  

1. The Bid Security shall be submitted as a Demand Draft (DD) for Rs. 5,00,000/- in favour of SAIL Refractory Company Limited, payable at Salem, or through NEFT.  

2. Alternatively, a "Bid Securing Declaration" (Appendix-1) may be submitted in lieu of the Bid Security.  

3. The Bid Security/Declaration shall be submitted online through the EPS portal along with the tender documents.  

4. Tenders received without the Bid Security or valid Bid Securing Declaration shall be rejected.  

5. The Bid Security of unsuccessful bidders shall be returned within 30 days from the date of award.  

6. The Bid Security of the successful bidder shall be retained until the Performance Guarantee is furnished.  

7. In case of withdrawal or modification of the bid after submission, the Bid Security shall be forfeited.  

8. Failure to sign the contract, if awarded, shall result in forfeiture of the Bid Security.  

9. The Bid Security shall be free of encumbrances and valid for a period exceeding the bid validity by 30 days.  

10. Any discrepancy in the Bid Security details may lead to disqualification.
