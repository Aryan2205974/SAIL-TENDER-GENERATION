**Bid Preparation**  

1. The tenderer shall download the tender documents free of cost from the SAIL website www.sailtenders.co.in (Plant: SAIL Refractory Company Limited).  

2. The complete tender documents, along with the Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) of Rs. 5,00,000/- (separate Demand Draft) in favour of SAIL Refractory Company Limited, payable at Salem, or through NEFT, or a “Bid Securing Declaration” (Appendix-1), shall be submitted online via the EPS portal.  

3. Tenderers must quote their best rates, as no further opportunity for negotiation will be provided. Negotiation, if deemed necessary, will be conducted exclusively with the lowest quoted tenderer.  

4. Tenders received after the specified date and time will be summarily rejected.  

5. The tenderer shall ensure that all documents are complete and submitted in accordance with the instructions provided in the tender document.  

6. Any discrepancies or incomplete submissions may lead to disqualification of the bid.  

7. The tenderer is responsible for ensuring the accuracy and authenticity of all submitted documents.  

8. Amendments or modifications to the tender, if any, will be communicated through the SAIL website, and tenderers must acknowledge such changes.  

9. The tenderer shall comply with all terms and conditions specified in the tender document and its annexures.  

10. Failure to adhere to the instructions provided herein may result in the rejection of the tender.
