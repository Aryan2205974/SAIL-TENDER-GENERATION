**Bid Validity**  

1. The bid shall remain valid for a period as specified in the tender schedule from the date of bid opening.  

2. Bids submitted with a shorter validity period shall be treated as non-responsive and rejected.  

3. The Purchaser reserves the right to request an extension of the bid validity period if deemed necessary.  

4. Bidders shall be notified in writing of any such extension request and may confirm or decline the extension within the stipulated timeframe.  

5. Failure to respond to an extension request shall result in forfeiture of the bid security.  

6. Bidders agreeing to extend bid validity shall provide a revised bid security, if required, valid for the extended period.  

7. No bidder shall be permitted to modify or withdraw their bid after the bid opening date, except as permitted under the terms of the tender.  

8. In the event of a delay in award beyond the original bid validity period, the Purchaser may seek consent from the lowest evaluated bidder to extend validity before proceeding with the award.  

9. The bid validity period shall be binding, and any bid found non-compliant shall be summarily rejected.  

10. The Purchaser reserves the right to annul the bidding process at any stage without liability to the bidders.
