**Award of Contract**  

1. The contract shall be awarded to the tenderer quoting the lowest evaluated price, subject to compliance with all terms and conditions of the tender.  

2. Negotiation, if deemed necessary, shall be conducted only with the lowest quoted tenderer. No further opportunity for negotiation shall be provided to other tenderers.  

3. The award of the contract shall be subject to the tenderer’s fulfillment of all eligibility criteria and submission of required documents as specified in the tender.  

4. SAIL reserves the right to accept or reject any or all tenders without assigning any reason whatsoever.  

5. The successful tenderer shall be notified in writing through the EPS portal or other official communication channels.  

6. The contract shall be deemed to be effective only upon issuance of the formal Letter of Award (LoA) by SAIL.  

7. The tenderer shall be required to confirm acceptance of the LoA within the stipulated time frame, failing which the offer shall stand forfeited.  

8. In case of any discrepancy between the tender documents and the LoA, the terms of the LoA shall prevail.  

9. The contract shall be governed by the terms and conditions specified in the tender documents and any subsequent amendments issued by SAIL.  

10. Any disputes arising out of the contract shall be resolved as per the arbitration clause specified in the tender documents.
