**SUBSECTION**  
**Amendments**  

1. The Purchaser reserves the right to modify or amend the tender documents at any time before the deadline for submission of bids.  

2. Any amendments issued shall be communicated in writing to all prospective bidders who have obtained the tender documents.  

3. Bidders shall acknowledge receipt of amendments in writing within the stipulated timeframe.  

4. Failure to acknowledge amendments may result in disqualification.  

5. Amendments shall form an integral part of the tender documents and shall be binding on all bidders.  

6. No oral explanations or instructions shall modify the terms of the tender unless confirmed in writing by the Purchaser.  

7. Bidders shall submit revised bids incorporating all amendments, if required.  

8. The Purchaser shall not be responsible for any delay or non-receipt of amendments by the bidder.  

9. Amendments may include extensions to the bid submission deadline, if deemed necessary by the Purchaser.  

10. No claim shall be entertained for any loss or expense incurred due to amendments.
