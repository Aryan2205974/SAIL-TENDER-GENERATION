**3.3 Unloading Requirements**  

3.3.1 The bidder shall be responsible for unloading the Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks at the designated location within Bokaro Steel Plant, as specified in the delivery schedule.  

3.3.2 Unloading shall be carried out using suitable equipment to prevent damage to the bricks. Manual handling, if required, shall be performed under strict supervision.  

3.3.3 The bidder shall ensure that unloading is completed within the stipulated time frame to avoid demurrage or detention charges, if applicable.  

3.3.4 All unloading activities shall comply with Bokaro Steel Plant’s safety and operational guidelines. The bidder shall provide necessary safety gear and equipment for personnel involved.  

3.3.5 The bidder shall arrange for adequate labor and machinery for timely unloading, ensuring no disruption to plant operations.  

3.3.6 Damaged or improperly unloaded materials shall be replaced by the bidder at no additional cost to the purchaser.  

3.3.7 The bidder shall coordinate with Bokaro Steel Plant’s logistics team for unloading permissions and site access.  

3.3.8 Unloading shall be conducted during working hours unless prior approval is obtained for extended timings.  

3.3.9 The bidder shall clear all debris and packaging materials post-unloading, leaving the site clean and hazard-free.  

3.3.10 Any delays or deviations from unloading requirements shall be promptly communicated to the purchaser for resolution.
