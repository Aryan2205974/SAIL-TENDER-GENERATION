**SUBSECTION**  

**Delivery Location**  

1. The delivered Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks shall be transported to the designated storage yard at Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro, as specified in the tender schedule.  

2. The Bidder shall ensure proper handling and unloading of materials at the delivery location without causing damage or delays.  

3. The exact delivery point within Bokaro Steel Plant shall be communicated post-award through a detailed delivery schedule.  

4. The Bidder shall coordinate with Bokaro Steel Plant’s logistics team for necessary entry permissions and unloading assistance.  

5. Delivery shall comply with all site-specific safety and security protocols of Bokaro Steel Plant.  

6. The Bidder shall bear all risks and costs associated with transportation until final acceptance at the delivery location.  

7. Any deviations from the designated delivery location must be pre-approved in writing by the Purchaser.  

8. The Bidder shall provide advance notice of shipment arrival to facilitate smooth receipt and inspection.  

9. Storage arrangements post-delivery, if required, shall be the Bidder’s responsibility unless otherwise agreed.  

10. Final delivery confirmation shall be subject to verification by Bokaro Steel Plant’s authorized representative.
