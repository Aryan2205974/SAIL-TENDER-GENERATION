**5. TRANSPORTATION REQUIREMENTS**  

5.1 The bidder shall ensure safe and secure transportation of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks to the designated location at Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro, with proper packing to prevent damage during transit.  

5.2 The bricks shall be transported in sturdy, weatherproof packaging, clearly marked with handling instructions, material specifications, and consignment details.  

5.3 The bidder shall arrange for suitable transport vehicles to prevent breakage or degradation of refractory properties due to improper handling or exposure.  

5.4 All transportation costs, including loading, unloading, and transit insurance, shall be borne by the bidder unless otherwise specified in the tender schedule.  

5.5 The consignment shall be accompanied by necessary transport documents, including delivery challans, material test certificates, and packing lists.  

5.6 The bidder shall comply with all applicable statutory regulations related to the transportation of refractory materials, including permits and safety standards.  

5.7 Partial deliveries will not be accepted unless explicitly authorized in writing by the purchaser.  

5.8 The bidder shall coordinate with Bokaro Steel Plant’s logistics team for delivery scheduling and gate entry permissions.  

5.9 Any damage or loss during transit shall be the bidder’s responsibility, and replacement shall be made without additional cost to the purchaser.  

5.10 Final delivery location and unloading instructions shall be as per the purchaser’s directives.
