**SUBSECTION: RISK TRANSFER**  

1. Risk of loss or damage to the Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks shall transfer to the Purchaser upon receipt at the designated delivery location at Bokaro Steel Plant.  

2. The Supplier shall bear all risks associated with transportation, handling, and storage until final acceptance by the Purchaser’s authorized representative.  

3. Any transit-related damages or shortages must be reported in writing within 48 hours of delivery, supported by documentary evidence.  

4. The Supplier shall indemnify the Purchaser against all claims arising from third-party liabilities during transit or unloading.  

5. Risk for non-conforming materials shall remain with the Supplier until rectification or replacement as per contractual terms.  

6. Force majeure events affecting risk transfer shall be governed by the general conditions of contract.  

7. The Supplier shall ensure proper packaging and labeling to mitigate risks during handling and storage.  

8. Partial deliveries shall not constitute complete risk transfer unless explicitly accepted by the Purchaser.  

9. The Purchaser reserves the right to reject materials if risk transfer conditions are not met.  

10. Insurance coverage for transit risks shall be the Supplier’s responsibility unless otherwise specified in the tender schedule.
