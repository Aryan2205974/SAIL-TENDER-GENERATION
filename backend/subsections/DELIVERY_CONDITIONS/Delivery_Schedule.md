**DELIVERY SCHEDULE**  

1. The supplier shall adhere to the delivery timeline as stipulated in the Purchase Order.  

2. Partial deliveries may be permitted subject to prior written approval from the Purchaser.  

3. The supplier shall submit a detailed delivery schedule within seven (7) days of order placement.  

4. Any delay in delivery beyond the agreed schedule shall be communicated in writing with justification.  

5. The supplier shall ensure all consignments are properly packed, labeled, and accompanied by necessary documentation.  

6. Delivery shall be made strictly as per the specified incoterms mentioned in the commercial terms.  

7. The supplier shall provide advance intimation of dispatch at least five (5) working days prior to arrival.  

8. Risk of loss or damage shall remain with the supplier until final acceptance at the designated location.  

9. The Purchaser reserves the right to revise the delivery schedule with mutual agreement.  

10. Non-compliance with the delivery schedule may attract penalties as per tender terms.
