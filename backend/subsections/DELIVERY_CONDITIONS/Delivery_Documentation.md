**DELIVERY DOCUMENTATION**  

1. The Supplier shall furnish a detailed packing list in triplicate, indicating the description, quantity, and identification marks of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for each consignment.  

2. A Material Test Certificate (MTC) from the manufacturer, certifying compliance with the specified technical parameters, shall accompany each shipment.  

3. The Supplier shall provide a Bill of Lading (BL) or Railway Receipt (RR) for road/rail consignments, duly endorsed for Bokaro Steel Plant’s receipt.  

4. A Certificate of Origin shall be submitted for imported materials, if applicable, as per statutory requirements.  

5. The Supplier shall include a dispatch advice note with each consignment, specifying the Purchase Order number, item description, and quantity.  

6. Hazardous material documentation, including Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS), shall be provided where applicable.  

7. The Supplier shall ensure all documents bear clear reference to the contract number, item code, and delivery schedule.  

8. Any customs clearance documents, including duty payment proof, shall be furnished for imported materials.  

9. The Supplier shall submit an undertaking confirming the accuracy and completeness of all supplied documentation.  

10. Failure to provide complete documentation may result in rejection of the consignment at the Buyer’s discretion.
