3. The Supplier shall provide necessary assistance, including access to records, test certificates, and relevant documentation, to verify compliance with the technical specifications.

6. The Supplier shall furnish certified test reports from recognized laboratories for each consignment, covering all specified parameters.  

7. SAIL may engage third-party inspection agencies, and the Supplier shall bear the associated costs if non-conformities are detected.  

8. The Supplier shall rectify any defects identified during inspection within the stipulated timeframe, failing which SAIL may impose penalties as per contract terms.
