**SPECIAL CONDITIONS OF CONTRACT**  

**SUBSECTION: SPECIAL DOCUMENTATION**  

1. The bidder shall submit a detailed technical datasheet of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks, including chemical composition, physical properties, and thermal characteristics, as per ASTM/ISO standards.  

2. Manufacturer’s test certificates for raw materials and finished products shall be furnished, certifying compliance with specified refractory standards.  

3. The bidder shall provide a quality assurance plan outlining inspection protocols, sampling methods, and testing procedures to ensure conformity with tender specifications.  

4. A documented process control plan shall be submitted, detailing production stages, quality checkpoints, and non-conformance handling mechanisms.  

5. The bidder shall include a material safety data sheet (MSDS) for the supplied bricks, covering handling, storage, and disposal guidelines.  

6. Proof of successful past supply of similar refractory bricks to integrated steel plants, supported by customer references, shall be provided.  

7. The bidder shall submit a packing list and transportation plan ensuring safe transit, including moisture-proofing measures, if applicable.  

8. Any deviations from the tender specifications must be explicitly declared, along with technical justifications for acceptance.  

9. The bidder shall provide a post-supply technical support proposal, including troubleshooting assistance and refractory lining recommendations.  

10. Failure to submit any mandatory documentation shall render the bid non-responsive.
