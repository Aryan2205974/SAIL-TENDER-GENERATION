**SPECIAL CONDITIONS OF CONTRACT**  

**Quality Compliance**  

1. The bidder shall supply Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks strictly conforming to the technical specifications provided in the tender documents. Any deviation shall render the bid liable for rejection.  

2. The bricks must meet the chemical composition, physical properties, and refractory performance standards as specified in the relevant IS/ISO/ASTM standards or as detailed in the technical schedule.  

3. The supplier shall provide a certificate of analysis/test reports from a NABL-accredited laboratory for each consignment, verifying compliance with the stipulated quality parameters.  

4. Raw materials used in manufacturing shall be of consistent quality, and the bidder shall submit mill certificates or material traceability documents upon request.  

5. The bricks shall be free from defects such as cracks, laminations, or impurities affecting performance in ladle applications.  

6. SAIL reserves the right to conduct third-party inspection or testing at the supplier’s premises or any stage of delivery, at the bidder’s cost, if quality non-conformance is suspected.  

7. Non-compliant material shall be rejected, and the supplier shall replace it at no additional cost within the agreed delivery period.  

8. The bidder shall furnish a detailed quality control plan, including process controls, inspection stages, and testing methodology, for approval before order execution.  

9. The supplier shall ensure proper packing to prevent transit damage, with each package marked with batch number, grade, and manufacturing date.  

10. Final acceptance shall be subject to satisfactory performance during trial usage at Bokaro Steel Plant.
