**SUBSECTION: BID SUBMISSION**  

1. Sealed bids shall be submitted under the Two-Bid system (Technical Bid and Price Bid) in separate sealed envelopes, clearly marked as such.  

2. Bids must be submitted in the prescribed format, duly signed and stamped by an authorized representative of the bidder.  

3. The outer envelope shall bear the tender reference number, due date, and the words “Bid for Procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application.”  

4. Bids received after the specified deadline shall be summarily rejected.  

5. All corrections in the bid documents must be initialed by the authorized signatory.  

6. Bidders shall ensure compliance with the technical specifications and commercial terms as stipulated in the tender documents.  

7. Any bid not conforming to the submission requirements shall be treated as non-responsive.  

8. Electronic submission of bids, if permitted, shall follow the guidelines provided in the tender schedule.  

9. Bidders shall provide a self-attested copy of the relevant documents supporting eligibility criteria.  

10. The bidder’s name, address, and contact details must be clearly mentioned on the bid submission envelope.
