**NOTICE INVITING TENDER**  

**Introduction**  

1. Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL), Bokaro Steel Plant, invites sealed tenders under the Two-Bid system from eligible and experienced bidders for the **Procurement of Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks for SMS – II Ladle application**.  

2. The tender documents shall be available for download from the SAIL tenders portal **(https://www.sailtenders.co.in)** from the date of publication of this notice.  

3. The cost of tender documents, if applicable, shall be as specified in the tender schedule.  

4. Interested bidders must submit their offers in the prescribed format, ensuring compliance with all technical and commercial specifications.  

5. The last date and time for receipt of tenders shall be as per the schedule mentioned in the tender documents. Late submissions shall not be considered.  

6. The tender opening shall take place at the designated venue as specified in the tender schedule.  

7. Bidders must ensure that all requisite documents, including Earnest Money Deposit (EMD), are submitted as per the tender conditions.  

8. Any amendments or corrigenda to the tender shall be notified through the SAIL tenders portal, and bidders must acknowledge the same.  

9. SAIL reserves the right to accept or reject any or all tenders without assigning any reason.  

10. For further clarifications, bidders may refer to the contact details provided in the tender documents.
