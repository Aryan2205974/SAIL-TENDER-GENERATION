**Bid Opening**  

1. The tender shall be opened on the specified date and time as mentioned in the tender notice.  

2. The venue for tender opening shall be as indicated in the tender document.  

3. Only authorized representatives of the tenderers may attend the tender opening process.  

4. The tender opening shall be conducted in the presence of the tender committee and representatives of the tenderers, if present.  

5. The tender opening process shall commence with the verification of the Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) and other mandatory documents.  

6. The technical bids shall be opened first, followed by the financial bids of technically qualified tenderers.  

7. The details of the tenderers and their quoted prices shall be recorded in the minutes of the tender opening meeting.  

8. The tender committee reserves the right to postpone or cancel the tender opening process without assigning any reason.  

9. The results of the tender opening shall be communicated to the tenderers in due course as per the tender schedule.  

10. Any deviation from the tender opening process shall be at the sole discretion of the tender authority.
