**Important Dates**  

1. The tender documents shall be available for download from the SAIL website, https://www.sailtenders.co.in, from the date specified in the tender schedule.  

2. The last date and time for receipt of sealed tenders shall be as per the tender schedule.  

3. The date and time for opening of tenders shall be specified in the tender schedule.  

4. Tenders received after the stipulated due date and time shall be summarily rejected and not considered for opening.  

5. The venue for submission of tender documents and tender opening shall be as per the tender schedule.  

6. Tenders submitted by fax or email shall not be accepted or entertained.  

7. Tenders not meeting the requirement of multiple sealed bid parts, as stipulated, shall be summarily rejected.  

8. No post-bid clarifications at the initiative of the bidder shall be entertained.  

9. The Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) and Security Deposit details shall be specified in the tender schedule.  

10. The cost of tender papers, if applicable, shall be mentioned in the tender schedule.
