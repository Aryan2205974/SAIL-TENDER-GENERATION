**INSTRUCTIONS FOR PARTICIPATION**  

1. **Tender Document Access**: Tender documents shall be downloaded exclusively from SAIL’s official tendering portal (https://www.sailtenders.co.in) from the date of publication. No physical copies will be issued.  

2. **Earnest Money Deposit (EMD)**: Bidders must submit EMD as specified in the tender schedule. Failure to submit EMD in the prescribed format shall render the bid non-responsive.  

3. **Bid Submission**: Sealed bids under the Two-Bid system (Technical & Commercial) shall be submitted to the designated tender box at the specified venue before the deadline. Late bids will be rejected.  

4. **Bid Opening**: Technical bids will be opened on the date and time indicated in the tender notice. Authorized representatives of bidders may attend the opening at their own expense.  

5. **Eligibility Criteria**: Bidders must meet the technical and financial eligibility conditions outlined in the tender documents. Non-compliance will lead to disqualification.  

6. **Clarifications**: Queries regarding tender terms must be submitted in writing to the designated authority within the stipulated period. Verbal communications will not be entertained.  

7. **Amendments**: SAIL reserves the right to modify/cancel the tender process at any stage without assigning reasons. Amendments will be notified through the official portal.  

8. **Taxes & Levies**: Quoted prices shall be exclusive of applicable taxes, duties, and levies, which will be paid as per statutory requirements.  

9. **Validity**: Bids must remain valid for the period specified in the tender schedule. Premature withdrawal will result in forfeiture of EMD.  

10. **Compliance**: Bidders must strictly adhere to all instructions in the tender documents. Deviations may lead to rejection.
