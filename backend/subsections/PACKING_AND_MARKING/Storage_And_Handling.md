**PACKING AND MARKING**  

**Storage And Handling**  

1. The bricks shall be packed in wooden crates or pallets with moisture-proof lining to prevent contamination during transit and storage.  

2. Each packing unit shall be clearly marked with the following details:  
   a) Manufacturer’s name and logo  
   b) Material designation (Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks)  
   c) Batch/lot number  
   d) Gross and net weight  

3. Storage conditions shall be dry and well-ventilated, free from direct exposure to weather elements. Stacking height shall not exceed manufacturer-recommended limits to prevent damage.  

4. Handling shall be performed using forklifts or cranes with appropriate lifting mechanisms to avoid impact or breakage.  

5. Any damaged or improperly packed material shall be rejected and replaced at the supplier’s cost.  

6. Special handling instructions, if any, shall be prominently displayed on each packing unit.  

7. The supplier shall ensure compatibility of packing materials with long-term storage requirements.  

8. No loose packing or unprotected edges shall be permitted.  

9. All markings shall be legible and resistant to fading under normal storage conditions.
