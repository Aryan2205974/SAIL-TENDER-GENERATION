**Marking Requirements**

1. Each brick shall be marked with the manufacturer’s name or logo, batch number, and date of manufacture.  

2. The outer packaging shall bear the following details:  
   a. Description of material (Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks).  
   b. Quantity in numbers.  
   c. Gross and net weight.  
   d. Purchase order number.  
   e. Destination (Bokaro Steel Plant, Bokaro).  

3. All markings shall be legible, permanent, and resistant to handling and transportation conditions.  

4. The packaging shall include a label indicating the handling instructions, including “Fragile” and “Handle with Care.”  

5. Each consignment shall be accompanied by a packing list detailing the contents, batch numbers, and corresponding quantities.  

6. The markings shall comply with international standards for material identification and traceability.  

7. Any additional marking requirements specific to the material or application shall be as per the tender schedule.  

8. In case of multiple packages, each package shall be sequentially numbered for easy identification and verification.  

9. The supplier shall ensure that all markings are clearly visible and not obscured during transportation or storage.  

10. Non-compliance with marking requirements shall be grounds for rejection of the consignment.
