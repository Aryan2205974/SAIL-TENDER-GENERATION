**Packing Requirements**  

1. The Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks shall be packed in sturdy, weatherproof, and corrosion-resistant packaging to ensure protection against transit damage, moisture, and contamination.  

2. Each brick shall be individually wrapped in polythene or equivalent material to prevent abrasion and chipping during handling and transportation.  

3. Bricks shall be packed in wooden crates or pallets with proper cushioning material to minimize breakage. The packing material shall be non-reactive and free from impurities.  

4. The gross weight of each packed unit shall not exceed the permissible limit for safe handling, as per standard industrial practices.  

5. All packing materials shall comply with applicable environmental regulations and must not contain hazardous substances.  

6. The supplier shall ensure proper stacking and securing of packed units to prevent shifting or collapse during transit.  

7. Inner packaging shall include moisture-absorbent desiccants where necessary to prevent dampness-related degradation.  

8. The packing method shall facilitate easy unloading, inspection, and handling at the destination without requiring specialized equipment.  

9. Any specific packing instructions mandated by the manufacturer for preserving brick quality shall be strictly followed.
