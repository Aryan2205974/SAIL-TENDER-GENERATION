**5. ACCEPTANCE AND REJECTION CRITERIA**  

5.1 The bricks shall be inspected and tested as per the specifications and standards referenced in the tender. Any deviation shall be grounds for rejection.  

5.2 Physical and chemical properties shall conform to the stipulated requirements. Non-conforming batches shall be rejected.  

5.3 Dimensional tolerances shall be verified as per applicable standards. Bricks outside permissible limits shall not be accepted.  

5.4 Visual defects such as cracks, chips, or uneven surfaces exceeding acceptable limits shall result in rejection.  

5.5 If test results fail to meet specified parameters, the entire lot represented by the sample shall be liable for rejection.  

5.6 The Purchaser reserves the right to conduct random sampling and testing at any stage of manufacturing or supply.  

5.7 Rejected material shall be replaced by the Supplier at no additional cost within the agreed timeframe.  

5.8 Final acceptance shall be subject to the Purchaser’s approval based on inspection reports and test certificates.  

5.9 The Supplier shall rectify or replace non-conforming materials promptly upon notification.  

5.10 Disputes regarding rejection shall be resolved as per the terms of the contract.
