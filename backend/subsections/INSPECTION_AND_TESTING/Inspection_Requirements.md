**Inspection Requirements**  

1. The bidder shall provide third-party inspection certificates from recognized agencies for Alumina Magnesia Spinel Bricks, covering chemical composition, physical properties, and dimensional accuracy.  

2. Manufacturer’s test reports for each batch shall be submitted, including results for bulk density, apparent porosity, cold crushing strength, and refractoriness under load.  

3. Random sampling of bricks shall be conducted as per IS 1528 or equivalent international standards, with samples tested at an accredited laboratory.  

4. Visual inspection shall be performed to verify absence of cracks, laminations, and surface defects exceeding permissible limits.  

5. Refractory lining dimensions and tolerances shall be verified against approved drawings and specifications.  

6. Pre-dispatch inspection shall be carried out at the manufacturer’s premises, with prior intimation to the purchaser.  

7. Rejection criteria shall include non-conformance to specified technical parameters, improper packaging, or damage during transit.  

8. The purchaser reserves the right to conduct surprise inspections at the supplier’s facility without prior notice.  

9. Final acceptance shall be subject to satisfactory performance during trial runs at the Bokaro Steel Plant.  

10. Non-compliant materials shall be replaced by the supplier at no additional cost within the stipulated timeframe.
