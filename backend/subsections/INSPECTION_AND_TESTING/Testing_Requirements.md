**Testing Requirements**  

1. The bidder shall furnish test certificates from a recognized laboratory for alumina magnesia spinel bricks, including chemical composition, physical properties, and refractory characteristics.  

2. All tests shall be conducted as per IS 1528 (Part 12) or equivalent international standards (ASTM/AISE).  

3. The bricks must undergo cold crushing strength (CCS) testing, with results conforming to the specified refractory grade.  

4. Reheat change tests shall be performed to determine dimensional stability at operating temperatures.  

5. The supplier shall provide test reports for thermal shock resistance, demonstrating suitability for ladle applications.  

6. Petrographic analysis shall be conducted to verify microstructure homogeneity and absence of defects.  

7. Refractoriness under load (RUL) testing shall be mandatory, with results meeting the required load-bearing capacity.  

8. The bidder shall submit a detailed testing methodology, including sample preparation and testing frequency.  

9. Third-party inspection may be conducted at the manufacturer’s premises, with costs borne by the supplier.  

10. Test failures shall result in rejection of the entire lot, with replacement at no additional cost to the purchaser.
